Aeldari Technology and Weapons

Advanced Technology Post-Fall Active

Overview

Aeldari technology represents one of the most sophisticated technological achievements in the galaxy, combining advanced materials science with psychic engineering. Their equipment seamlessly integrates the material and the psychic, creating weapons and tools that appear as magic to less advanced races.
