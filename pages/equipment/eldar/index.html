Eldar Equipment

Personal Equipment

Aspect Armor

Protection: High

Mobility: Exceptional

Special: Psychically-attuned

Highly sophisticated armor worn by Aspect Warriors, each suit is attuned to its wearer and enhances their natural abilities. The armor's systems are psychically activated.

Shuriken Weapons

Type: Projectile Weapon

Rate of Fire: Very High

Ammunition: Monomolecular Discs

Advanced weapons firing razor-sharp discs at incredible velocities. The monomolecular edges can slice through almost any armor with ease.

Wraithbone Equipment

Material: Psychic Construct

Durability: Self-repairing

Special: Psychically Reactive

Living psychic material that forms the basis of all Eldar technology. Wraithbone can be shaped by thought and is incredibly resilient while remaining lightweight.

Vehicles

Wave Serpent

Type: Grav-Tank Transport

Speed: Very High

Protection: Energy Shield

Capacity: 12 Warriors

Advanced transport vehicle utilizing anti-gravitic technology and equipped with powerful energy shields. Can rapidly deploy Aspect Warriors while providing fire support.

Fire Prism

Type: Heavy Grav-Tank

Armament: Prism Cannon

Special: Linked Fire Capability

Sophisticated battle tank armed with the devastating Prism Cannon. Multiple Fire Prisms can link their fire for exponentially increased destructive power.

Falcon Grav Tank

Type: Multi-role Grav-Tank

Speed: High

Armament: Pulse Laser, Scatter Laser

Versatile combat vehicle combining transport capacity with impressive firepower. Its sophisticated targeting systems allow it to engage multiple targets while moving at full speed.

Ships

Void Stalker Battleship

Class: Battleship

Length: 15km

Special: Holofields

Armament: Pulsar Lances

The most powerful vessels in the Eldar fleet, Void Stalkers combine incredible speed with devastating firepower. Their holofields make them nearly impossible to target effectively.

Shadow Class Cruiser

Class: Light Cruiser

Length: 5km

Special: Advanced Stealth Systems

Stealthy vessels that excel at reconnaissance and surgical strikes. Their advanced sensor systems can detect enemy ships long before being detected themselves.

Eclipse Class Cruiser

Class: Heavy Cruiser

Length: 10km

Special: Advanced Shielding

Armament: Pulsar Lances, Star Cannons

Powerful and versatile cruisers that serve as the backbone of the Eldar fleet. Their advanced shielding and firepower make them a formidable opponent in any engagement.

Wraithship

Class: Specialist Vessel

Length: Variable

Special: Living Construction

Mysterious vessels grown from wraithbone and guided by the spirits of dead Eldar. These ships can repair themselves and respond to psychic commands.
