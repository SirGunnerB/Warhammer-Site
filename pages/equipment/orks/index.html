Ork Equipment

Personal Equipment

Shoota

Type: Ballistic Weapon

Reliability: Variable

Dakka Rating: High

Standard Ork automatic weapon that embodies their philosophy of "more dakka". Crude but effective, firing large-caliber rounds at a devastating rate.

Power Klaw

Type: Power Weapon

Strength: Extreme

Field Type: Disruption

Massive powered claw capable of ripping through armor and vehicles. Favored by Ork Nobz and Warbosses for its devastating close combat potential.

Mega Armour

Type: Heavy Armor

Protection: Extreme

Mobility: Limited

Incredibly thick armor cobbled together from various sources. While slow, it makes the wearer nearly invulnerable to small arms fire.

Vehicles

Battlewagon

Type: Heavy Transport

Armor: Heavy

Speed: Variable

Massive war vehicle that serves as both transport and mobile fortress. Each is unique, customized with additional armor, weapons, and "kustom bits".

Deff Dread

Type: Walker

Armament: Customizable

Pilot: Cybork

Walking war machine piloted by a hardwired Ork. Armed with multiple close combat weapons and ranged options for maximum destruction.

Trukk

Type: Light Transport

Speed: Fast

Durability: Moderate

Fast but ramshackle transport vehicle. Essential for getting Boyz into combat quickly, often decorated with tribal symbols and trophies.

Ships

Rok

Type: Assault Ship

Size: Massive

Method: Impact

Hollowed-out asteroid converted into a crude but effective spacecraft. Used for both transportation and as a weapon by crashing into planets.

Kill Kroozer

Type: Heavy Warship

Armament: Heavy

Crew: Large

Large Ork warship bristling with guns and launching bays. While crude by Imperial standards, they are surprisingly effective in combat.

Savage Gunship

Type: Attack Craft

Speed: Very Fast

Durability: Light

Fast attack vessel used for hit-and-run attacks and boarding actions. Often equipped with multiple rocket pods and big shootas.
