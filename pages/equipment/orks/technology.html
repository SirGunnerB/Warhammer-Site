Ork Technology and Equipment

Orkish Engineering WAAAGH! Technology Perpetually "Improved"

Overview

Ork technology represents a unique phenomenon in the galaxy, where the collective belief of the Orks, known as the "WAAAGH! field," actually influences the functionality of their equipment. Their technology often defies conventional physics and engineering principles, working primarily because the Orks believe it should work.
