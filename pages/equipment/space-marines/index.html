Personal Equipment

Mark X Tacticus Power Armor

Protection: Exceptional

Mobility: High

Power Supply: Fusion core

The latest iteration of Adeptus Astartes power armor, combining superior protection with enhanced mobility. Features integrated life support, auto-senses, and strength enhancement systems.

Bolter

Type: Rapid-fire weapon

Caliber: .75 caliber

Range: Effective up to 2.5km

The iconic weapon of the Space Marines, firing self-propelled explosive rounds. Each bolt is a miniature rocket containing mass-reactive explosives.

Chainsword

Type: Melee weapon

Power: Motorized chain teeth

Effectiveness: High against armor

A brutal close combat weapon featuring motorized teeth that tear through armor and flesh alike. Standard issue for many Space Marine officers.

Vehicles

Land Raider

Type: Main Battle Tank

Armor: Extremely Heavy

Capacity: 10 Space Marines

The Land Raider is the most powerful tank in the Space Marines' arsenal, combining exceptional protection with devastating firepower. Features twin-linked lascannons and heavy bolters.

Rhino APC

Type: Armored Personnel Carrier

Armor: Heavy

Capacity: 10 Space Marines

The workhorse of Space Marine forces, the Rhino provides protected transport for tactical squads. Reliable and easily maintained.

Ships

Battle Barge

Class: Capital Ship

Length: 8-12km

Armament: Heavy

The largest vessels in a Chapter's fleet, Battle Barges serve as mobile fortresses and command centers. They carry enormous firepower and can deploy entire companies of Space Marines.

Strike Cruiser

Class: Medium Cruiser

Length: 4.5km

Armament: Medium-Heavy

Fast and well-armed, Strike Cruisers are the backbone of Space Marine fleets. They excel at planetary assault operations and ship-to-ship combat.
