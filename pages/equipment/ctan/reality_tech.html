C'tan Technology and Reality Manipulation

Transcendent Technology War in Heaven Partially Active

Overview

The C'tan, or Star Gods, possessed technology and abilities that transcended the conventional laws of physics. Their mastery over reality itself, combined with their consumption of stars and manipulation of living metal, created some of the most formidable and incomprehensible technologies in the galaxy's history.
