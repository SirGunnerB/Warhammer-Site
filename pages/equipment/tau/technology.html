Overview

T'au technology represents some of the most advanced and rapidly evolving technological achievements in the galaxy. Their approach to scientific advancement, unhindered by superstition or religious doctrine, has produced sophisticated weapons and equipment that often surpass those of other races in efficiency and effectiveness.
