Chaos Technology and Warp Artifacts

Warp-Infused Technology Post-Heresy Active

Overview

Chaos technology represents a twisted fusion of material science and warp energy, where machinery and equipment become possessed by daemonic entities or warped by the powers of the immaterium. This unholy merger creates artifacts of immense power, though often at a terrible cost to their users.
