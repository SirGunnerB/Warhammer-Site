Defiler

Type: Daemon Engine

Armor: Very Heavy

A massive walking tank possessed by a bound daemon, combining heavy firepower with devastating close combat capabilities. Its spider-like legs allow it to traverse any terrain.

Heldrake

Type: Daemon Engine Flyer

Speed: Very High

Armament: Baleflamer/Hades Autocannon

A flying daemon engine that combines the worst aspects of dragon and machine. Capable of both aerial combat and ground attack missions.

Length: 8km

Special: Warp-corrupted

Class: Heavy Cruiser

Length: 5km

Fast and heavily armed cruisers that excel at void combat. Their weapons have been corrupted by the Warp, making them even more deadly than their Imperial counterparts.
