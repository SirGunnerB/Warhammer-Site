Artifacts and Equipment of the Old Ones

Ancient Technology Pre-War in Heaven Mostly Lost

Overview

The artifacts and equipment of the Old Ones represent the pinnacle of technological achievement in the galaxy's history. These items range from reality-altering devices to sophisticated biological manipulation tools, many of which remain beyond the comprehension of even the most advanced races in the 41st Millennium.
