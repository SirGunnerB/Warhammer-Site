Imperial Navy Equipment

Capital Ships

Emperor Class Battleship

Class: Battleship

Length: 8km

Crew: 141,000+

Main Armament: Nova Cannon, Lance Batteries

The Emperor Class Battleship is one of the most powerful vessels in the Imperial Navy. These mighty warships serve as flagships for Battlefleet commanders and can obliterate smaller vessels with ease.

Retribution Class Battleship

Class: Battleship

Length: 7.5km

Crew: 125,000+

Main Armament: Heavy Lance Batteries, Torpedo Tubes

The Retribution Class emphasizes firepower over all else, featuring massive broadside batteries capable of reducing enemy vessels to molten slag.

Cruisers

Gothic Class Cruiser

Class: Heavy Cruiser

Length: 5km

Crew: 95,000

Main Armament: Lance Batteries, Weapon Batteries

The Gothic Class Cruiser is a versatile vessel combining both lance weapons and conventional batteries, making it effective against both heavily armored and lighter targets.

Lunar Class Cruiser

Class: Light Cruiser

Length: 4.5km

Crew: 85,000

Main Armament: Weapon Batteries, Torpedo Tubes

The most numerous of Imperial cruiser classes, the Lunar Class is a jack-of-all-trades vessel capable of performing well in most combat situations.

Escort Vessels

Cobra Class Destroyer

Class: Destroyer

Length: 1.5km

Crew: 15,000

Main Armament: Torpedo Tubes, Light Weapons

Fast and maneuverable, Cobra Class Destroyers excel at harassment tactics and screening larger vessels. Their torpedo salvos can pose a serious threat to larger ships.

Fighter Craft

Fury Interceptor

Type: Space Superiority Fighter

Crew: 1-2

Armament: Las-cannons, Missiles

The primary fighter of the Imperial Navy, the Fury excels at both dogfighting and anti-ship strikes. Its superior speed and maneuverability make it a feared opponent.

Starhawk Bomber

Type: Heavy Bomber

Crew: 3-4

Armament: Heavy Bombs, Defensive Turrets

The Starhawk is designed to deliver devastating bombing runs against capital ships. While slower than fighters, its payload can cripple even the largest vessels.
