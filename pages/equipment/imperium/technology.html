Imperial Technology and Equipment

Military Technology Dark Millennium Active

Overview

Imperial technology represents a complex mixture of advanced capabilities and regression, where ancient technological marvels are maintained through ritual and tradition rather than understanding. The Adeptus Mechanicus serves as the primary keeper and manufacturer of Imperial technology, treating machines as divine manifestations of the Omnissiah.
