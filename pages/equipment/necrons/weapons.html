Necron Weapons and Technology

Advanced Technology War in Heaven Era Active

Overview

Necron technology represents some of the most advanced weaponry and equipment in the galaxy, developed during their war against the Old Ones and refined over millions of years. Their mastery of physical laws and manipulation of reality itself makes their technology appear as sorcery to lesser races.
