Necron Equipment

Personal Equipment

Gauss Flayer

Type: Gauss Weapon

Effect: Molecular Disassembly

Range: Long

Standard Necron weapon that strips away its target's atomic structure layer by layer. Even a glancing hit can cause catastrophic damage to both organic and inorganic matter.

Living Metal

Type: Self-repairing Material

Durability: Extreme

Special: Regenerative

The mysterious material that forms Necron bodies and equipment. Capable of repairing itself from almost any damage, making Necron warriors incredibly difficult to destroy permanently.

Phase Shifter

Type: Defense System

Effect: Dimensional Shift

Duration: Variable

Advanced technology that allows the user to exist partially out of phase with reality, causing most attacks to pass harmlessly through them.

Vehicles

Ghost Ark

Type: Transport/Repair Vehicle

Capacity: 10 Warriors

Special: Repair Protocols

Advanced transport that can repair and reassemble damaged Necron Warriors while providing mobile fire support. Hovers silently above the battlefield.

Doomsday Ark

Type: Heavy Support

Armament: Doomsday Cannon

Range: Extreme

Mobile artillery platform carrying the devastating Doomsday Cannon. Capable of obliterating even the heaviest tanks with a single shot.

Monolith

Type: Mobile Fortress

Armor: Living Metal

Special: Dimensional Gateway

Massive floating pyramid that serves as both a weapons platform and a dimensional portal. Can teleport Necron forces directly into battle.

Ships

Cairn Class Tombship

Class: Super-heavy Battleship

Length: 15km

Special: Self-repairing

The largest and most powerful vessels in the Necron fleet. These ancient ships carry planet-destroying weapons and can repair themselves from almost any damage.

Scythe Class Harvest Ship

Class: Cruiser

Length: 5km

Special: Harvest Beams

Specialized vessels equipped with devastating harvest beams that can strip entire planets of life. Often used to collect resources for Necron tomb worlds.

Shroud Class Light Cruiser

Class: Light Cruiser

Length: 3km

Special: Stealth Systems

Stealthy vessels that use advanced technology to remain undetected. Often used for reconnaissance and surgical strikes against specific targets.
