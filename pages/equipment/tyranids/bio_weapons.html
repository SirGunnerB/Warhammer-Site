Tyranid Bio-Weapons and Organic Technology

Bio-Organic Technology Constant Evolution Active

Overview

Tyranid bio-weapons represent the pinnacle of organic evolution and adaptation, where every organism is a perfectly engineered living weapon. The Hive Mind constantly refines and evolves these bio-constructs, creating increasingly deadly and efficient tools of war from the biomass of conquered worlds.
