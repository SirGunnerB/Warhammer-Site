The Old Ones' Weapons Technology

Military Technology Warfare Systems War in Heaven

Overview

The Old Ones developed some of the most sophisticated weapons systems ever known in the galaxy. Their arsenal combined conventional physics, psychic power, and dimensional engineering to create weapons of unprecedented power. During the War in Heaven, these weapons proved crucial in their conflict against the C'tan and their Necrontyr servants.
