The Old Ones' Mastery of the Warp

Advanced Knowledge Psychic Science Pre-War in Heaven

Overview

The Old Ones possessed an unparalleled understanding of the Warp, viewing it not as a mysterious realm of chaos but as a fundamental force that could be studied, understood, and manipulated. Their mastery of both material and immaterial realms allowed them to achieve technological and psychic feats that remain unmatched in the galaxy's history.
