Old Ones Technology

Ancient Technology Multiple Disciplines Largely Lost

Overview

The Old Ones possessed technology so advanced it was indistinguishable from magic to later civilizations. Their mastery of genetics, psychic powers, and dimensional engineering created wonders that remain unmatched even in the 41st Millennium. Their technological achievements formed the foundation for many of the galaxy's advanced civilizations.
