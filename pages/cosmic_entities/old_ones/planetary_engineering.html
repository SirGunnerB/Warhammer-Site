The Old Ones' Planetary Engineering

Advanced Technology World Shaping Pre-War in Heaven

Overview

The Old Ones' mastery of planetary engineering represented the pinnacle of environmental manipulation in galactic history. Their ability to reshape worlds, create sustainable ecosystems, and establish complex biospheres demonstrated an understanding of planetary sciences far beyond current comprehension. This mastery allowed them to create perfect environments for their created species and establish a vast network of life-bearing worlds.
