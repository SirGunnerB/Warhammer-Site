The Jokaero

Engineered Species Technology Masters War in Heaven to Present

Overview

The Jokaero are one of the most unique creations of the Old Ones, engineered to be living repositories of technological knowledge and master craftsmen. Despite their simian appearance, they possess an innate understanding of advanced technology that surpasses most other races in the galaxy. Their continued existence represents one of the most successful outcomes of the Old Ones' bio-engineering programs.
