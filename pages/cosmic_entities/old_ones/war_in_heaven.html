The War in Heaven

Ancient Conflict Galaxy-Spanning War Historical Event

Overview

The War in Heaven was the most catastrophic conflict in galactic history, fought approximately 60 million years before the current era. This war reshaped the galaxy, spawned new species, and led to the fall of the Old Ones' civilization. Its aftermath continues to influence events in the 41st Millennium.

Origins of the Conflict

Initial Causes

  • Necrontyr's short, painful lives
  • Old Ones' refusal to share immortality
  • Political and territorial tensions
  • Technological disparities

The Necrontyr's First Strike

The Necrontyr, bitter at their short, radiation-plagued lives, launched a war against the Old Ones. Despite their technological advancement, they were unable to overcome the Old Ones' mastery of the Webway and psychic powers.

The C'tan Alliance

The Star Gods' Arrival

In their desperation, the Necrontyr discovered and made contact with the C'tan, offering them physical forms of living metal. This alliance would change the nature of the war dramatically.
