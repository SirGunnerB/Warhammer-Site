The Webway

Dimensional Network Inter-dimensional Travel Partially Functional

Overview

The Webway is a vast network of tunnels that exists between realspace and the Warp, created by the Old Ones and later inherited by the Aeldari. It serves as a safe means of interstellar travel and communication, protecting its users from the dangers of both normal space and the Warp while allowing near-instantaneous transport across vast distances.

Nature and Structure

Physical Characteristics

  • Labyrinthine tunnels
  • Non-Euclidean geometry
  • Crystalline construction
  • Self-repairing architecture

Dimensional Properties

The Webway exists in an artificial sub-dimension, protected from both the material universe and the Warp by sophisticated barriers. These barriers are created from an unknown material that can resist both physical and psychic intrusion.

Historical Development

Creation by the Old Ones

The Old Ones created the Webway as a means of instantaneous travel across their empire, using their mastery of both technology and psychic powers. It was their greatest achievement in terms of infrastructure and transportation.
