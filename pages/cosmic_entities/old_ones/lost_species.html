Lost Species of the Old Ones

Extinct/Lost Species Various Purposes War in Heaven Era

Overview

Beyond the well-known creations like the Aeldari and Krork, the Old Ones engineered numerous other species for specific purposes during the War in Heaven. Many of these species were lost to time, either through extinction during the conflict, the Enslaver Plague, or through evolution into unrecognizable forms. This archive documents what is known about these mysterious species.

The Rashan

Known Purpose

  • Webway maintenance and navigation
  • Dimensional engineering
  • Psychic communication networks
  • Reality stabilization

Recorded Abilities

  • Dimensional Manipulation
    • Webway path creation
    • Reality fabric repair
    • Dimensional anchoring
  • Psychic Capabilities
    • Long-range communication
    • Dimensional mapping
    • Warp current navigation

Theoretical Fate

The Rashan are believed to have either retreated deep into the Webway during the War in Heaven or evolved into purely energy-based beings, making their current existence impossible to verify.

The K'nib

Known Purpose

Recorded Abilities

  • Combat Capabilities
    • Advanced tactical coordination
    • Adaptive warfare techniques
    • Environmental manipulation
  • Defense Systems
    • Planetary shield generation
    • Strategic fortification

Theoretical Fate

Archaeological evidence suggests the K'nib may have been among the first casualties of the Enslaver Plague, with their defensive capabilities being overwhelmed by the psychic parasites.
