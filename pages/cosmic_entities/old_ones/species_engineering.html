Species Engineering: The Old Ones' Creation Methods

Advanced Technology Biological Engineering War in Heaven Era

Overview

The Old Ones' mastery of species engineering represented the pinnacle of biological and psychic science in the galaxy's history. Their methods combined advanced genetic manipulation, psychic engineering, and dimensional science to create species perfectly adapted to specific purposes. This knowledge, largely lost to time, produced some of the most significant races in the galaxy.
