The Old Ones

First Civilization Life and Evolution Extinct/Unknown

Overview

The Old Ones were the first major civilization to arise in the Milky Way galaxy, masters of both science and the Warp. Their advanced understanding of genetics, physics, and psychic powers allowed them to shape entire species and travel between stars with ease. Their legacy continues to influence galactic events even after their apparent extinction.

Nature & Characteristics

Physical Description

Little is known about the Old Ones' true appearance. Various accounts describe them as reptilian beings of great size and presence, though this may be metaphorical or representative of only one of their forms. Their mastery of genetics suggests they may have had multiple forms.

Known Capabilities

  • Genetic manipulation
  • Psychic mastery
  • Webway creation
  • Species uplifting

Technology & Achievements

Major Developments

  • The Webway network
  • Genetic engineering
  • Psychic technology
  • Warp manipulation

The Webway

Perhaps their greatest achievement, the Webway is a vast network of tunnels through the Warp, allowing for safe and rapid transit across the galaxy. The Aeldari later inherited much of this network, though they understand only a fraction of its true complexity.

Genetic Engineering

The Old Ones were masters of life manipulation, creating or uplifting numerous species for various purposes. Their understanding of genetics far surpassed anything achieved since.

The War in Heaven

The Conflict

The War in Heaven was a cataclysmic conflict between the Old Ones and the C'tan-led Necrontyr. This war reshaped the galaxy and led to the creation of many weapons and species that still influence events in the 41st millennium.

Major Events

  • Necrontyr rebellion
  • C'tan alliance
  • Creation of warrior races
  • Fall of the Old Ones

Aftermath

The war's conclusion saw the apparent extinction or departure of the Old Ones, though their legacy lived on through their creations and the technological marvels they left behind.

Various factions continue to study Old One artifacts and ruins, seeking to understand their technology and possibly their fate. The Aeldari maintain the most complete records, though much remains mysterious even to them.
