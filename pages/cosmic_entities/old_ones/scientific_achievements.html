Scientific Achievements of the Old Ones

Scientific Legacy Advanced Technology Pre-War in Heaven

Overview

The Old Ones achieved levels of scientific understanding that remain unmatched in the galaxy's history. Their mastery over life, matter, energy, and the fundamental forces of reality established them as the most technologically advanced species ever known. Their achievements span multiple scientific disciplines and continue to influence the galaxy in the 41st Millennium.
