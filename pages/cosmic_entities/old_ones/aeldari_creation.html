The Aeldari: Masters of the Psychic Arts

Engineered Species Psychic Warriors War in Heaven to Present

Overview

The Aeldari represent the pinnacle of the Old Ones' psychic engineering, created as a race of powerful psykers to serve as both warriors and stewards of the galaxy. Their sophisticated minds and powerful psychic abilities made them the most advanced of all the Old Ones' creations, leading to their eventual dominion over the galaxy in the wake of the War in Heaven.
