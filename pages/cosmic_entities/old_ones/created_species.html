Created Species

Engineered Races Multiple Species Various States

Overview

The Old Ones created numerous species through their mastery of genetic engineering and psychic manipulation. Each race was designed with specific purposes in mind, from warfare to technological advancement. Many of these species continue to play major roles in the galaxy of the 41st Millennium, though often in forms quite different from their original design.

The Aeldari

Original Purpose

  • Psychic warfare
  • Webway maintenance
  • Cultural preservation
  • Galactic stewardship

Engineered Traits

  • Extreme psychic sensitivity
  • Enhanced intelligence
  • Extended lifespan
  • Emotional intensity

Current Status

The Aeldari have fractured into several distinct groups following their Fall: Craftworld Aeldari, Drukhari, Harlequins, and Exodites. Each represents a different approach to surviving their species' near-extinction.

The Krork

Original Purpose

  • Warfare and combat
  • Self-replicating armies
  • Adaptive evolution
  • Biological resilience

Engineered Traits

  • Rapid reproduction
  • Innate combat knowledge
  • Fungal biology
  • Collective psychic field

Evolution into Orks

The Krork devolved into the modern Orks, losing much of their original sophistication while retaining their core traits of combat prowess and resilience. Despite this regression, they remain one of the most successful species in the galaxy.

The Jokaero

Original Purpose

  • Technology maintenance
  • Equipment creation
  • Technological preservation
  • Engineering support

Engineered Traits

  • Instinctive engineering
  • Advanced problem-solving
  • Digital manipulation
  • Technological intuition

Current Status

The Jokaero survive as a reclusive species, still maintaining their incredible technological abilities. They are sought after by various factions for their engineering capabilities.

The Rashan

Original Purpose

  • Psychic communication
  • Dimensional navigation
  • Webway maintenance

Known Traits

Little is known about the Rashan, though they are believed to have been created specifically for maintaining and navigating the Webway network.

The K'nib

Original Purpose

  • Specialized warfare
  • Planetary defense
  • Tactical operations

Known Information

The K'nib were one of several warrior species created during the War in Heaven. Their exact capabilities and fate remain largely unknown.
