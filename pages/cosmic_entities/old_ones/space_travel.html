The Old Ones' Space Travel Technology

Transportation Technology Space-Time Manipulation Pre-War in Heaven

Overview

The Old Ones achieved mastery over space-time travel through their profound understanding of dimensional physics and psychic science. Their most notable achievement was the creation of the Webway, but their transportation technologies extended far beyond this, encompassing various methods of faster-than-light travel and dimensional manipulation.
