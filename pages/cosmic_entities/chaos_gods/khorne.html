Khorne

The Blood God War, Blood, and Skulls Eternal

Overview

Khorne is the Chaos God of blood, war, and murder. He is the most powerful and martial of the Chaos Gods, embodying violence, rage, and martial pride. His eternal throne sits upon a mountain of skulls in the midst of a sea of blood, each skull taken in his name from the countless battlefields across time and space.

Manifestation

Physical Appearance

When manifested, Khorne appears as a towering figure of brutal martial might, seated upon a brass throne atop a mountain of skulls. His armor is ancient and scarred, and his head is that of a snarling, bestial creature.

Domain

His realm in the Warp is an endless plain of brass and bone, where rivers of blood flow between mountains of skulls and great fortresses of brass and iron echo with the sounds of eternal battle.
