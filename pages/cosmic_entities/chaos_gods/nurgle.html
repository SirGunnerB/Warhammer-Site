Nurgle

The Plague Lord Decay, Disease, and Rebirth Eternal

Overview

Nurgle, also known as the Plague Lord or Grandfather Nurgle, is the Chaos God of disease, decay, and rebirth. He represents the cycle of life and death, pestilence and perseverance. Despite his horrific aspect, he views himself as a loving grandfather figure to his followers, offering them relief from pain through acceptance of decay.

Manifestation

Physical Appearance

Appears as a massive, bloated figure covered in boils, lesions, and signs of every disease in existence. Despite his revolting appearance, his face often bears a grandfatherly smile, reflecting his paradoxical nature as both destroyer and creator.

Domain

His realm in the Warp is a rotting garden of decay and rebirth, where new diseases are cultivated and life springs eternal from death. His domain includes the infamous Garden of Nurgle, tended by his daemonic servants.

Mortal Followers

Those who seek relief from pain or fear of death, plague doctors, and those who embrace decay. His followers often form tight-knit, family-like groups, viewing themselves as Nurgle's beloved children.

The Garden of Nurgle
