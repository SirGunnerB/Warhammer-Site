Slaanesh

The Dark Prince Excess and Perfection Eternal

Overview

Slaanesh, known as the Dark Prince and the Prince of Pleasure, is the youngest of the Chaos Gods. Born from the fall of the Aeldari Empire, Slaanesh embodies the pursuit of perfection and excess in all its forms. The Dark Prince represents both the heights of artistic achievement and the depths of destructive obsession.

Manifestation

Physical Appearance

Slaanesh appears in whatever form is most alluring to the observer, often depicted as an aesthetically perfect being of supernatural grace. The true form is said to be both entrancing and terrifying to behold.

Domain

The Palace of Pleasure, Slaanesh's realm in the Warp, is a place of impossible beauty and endless temptation, where every desire is fulfilled only to reveal new, greater desires.

A realm of perfect beauty and endless temptation, where every circle draws visitors deeper into increasingly refined experiences. The architecture itself is a masterwork of aesthetic perfection.

The Aeldari Connection

The Fall

Slaanesh's birth during the Fall of the Aeldari Empire created the Eye of Terror and nearly destroyed their civilization. This event continues to shape the relationship between Slaanesh and the surviving Aeldari races.
