Tzeentch

The Architect of Fate Change and Sorcery Eternal

Overview

Tzeentch, known as the Architect of Fate and the Changer of Ways, is the Chaos God of change, evolution, intrigue, and sorcery. He is the most enigmatic and subtle of the Chaos Gods, weaving complex schemes that span millennia and orchestrating events across the galaxy through manipulation and magic.

Manifestation

Physical Appearance

Tzeentch's form is ever-changing and impossible to truly comprehend. He appears as a mass of constantly shifting shapes, colors, and patterns, with multiple faces and limbs that appear and disappear. His true form is said to drive mortals mad with its complexity.

Domain

His realm in the Warp is a labyrinthine dimension of crystalline structures and impossible geometries, where reality shifts constantly and magic flows like water. At its center lies the Impossible Fortress, which defies all logic and physics.

Mortal Followers

Sorcerers, scholars, conspirators, and those who seek forbidden knowledge or desire change. His followers often form secretive cults and cabals, each believing they are part of a greater plan.

The Impossible Fortress

Description

The heart of Tzeentch's realm, a structure that simultaneously exists and doesn't exist, containing infinite rooms within finite space. Its architecture constantly shifts and changes, making navigation impossible for all but the most powerful daemons.
