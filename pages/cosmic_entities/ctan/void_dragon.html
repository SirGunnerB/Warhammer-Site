The Void Dragon

C'tan Star God Technology and Physical Laws Sharded

Overview

The Void Dragon, also known as Mag'ladroth, is one of the most powerful C'tan Star Gods. Master of technology and physical laws, it is believed to be the most potent of all C'tan in terms of raw power. Ancient legends suggest a connection between the Void Dragon and the Omnissiah worshipped by the Adeptus Mechanicus, though such claims are considered heretical.

Manifestation

Physical Appearance

In its original form, the Void Dragon appeared as a massive serpentine creature of living metal, wreathed in lightning and dark energy. Its current sharded form maintains aspects of this appearance, though at a much reduced scale.

Domain

The Void Dragon's influence extends over technology, machinery, and the fundamental laws of physics. Its power over these domains is so complete that even its fragments can dominate vast technological systems.

Historical Impact

War in Heaven

During the War in Heaven, the Void Dragon was one of the most devastating weapons in the Necron arsenal. Its mastery over technology allowed it to shut down entire planetary defense systems and turn enemy machines against their makers.

The Great Sleep

Legends speak of an epic battle between the Emperor of Mankind and a great dragon on ancient Terra, resulting in the creature's imprisonment beneath Mars. Whether this was the Void Dragon itself or merely one of its shards remains a matter of debate.

The Mechanicum Connection

The Mars Theory

Some tech-priests theorize that the Omnissiah and the Dragon of Mars are one and the same, though such speculation is considered highly heretical. The influence of the Void Dragon may explain humanity's innate understanding of technology.

Known Shards

Notable Fragments

After the Necron rebellion, the Void Dragon was shattered into countless shards, each retaining a fraction of its original power. Some of these shards have been encountered throughout the galaxy.
