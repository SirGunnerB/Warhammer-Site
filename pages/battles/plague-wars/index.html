The War

The Plague Wars represented a massive invasion of Ultramar by the forces of Nurgle, led by the Daemon Primarch Mortarion and his Death Guard Legion.

The Corruption

The Death Guard's strategy focused on corrupting entire worlds with Nurgle's plagues, turning them into breeding grounds for new diseases and daemon engines. The Garden World of Iax was particularly devastated, transformed into a nightmarish realm of pestilence.

The Defense

Guilliman orchestrated a masterful defense of his realm, utilizing the combined strength of the Ultramarines, their successor chapters, and various Imperial forces. The campaign culminated in a direct confrontation between the two Primarchs.

Aftermath

While the Imperial forces emerged victorious, the cost was devastating. Many worlds of Ultramar would take decades to recover from the corruption of Nurgle, if they could be saved at all.

Major Consequences

  • Devastation of multiple worlds
  • Permanent corruption of Iax
  • Strengthening of Ultramar's defenses
  • Development of new anti-plague protocols
