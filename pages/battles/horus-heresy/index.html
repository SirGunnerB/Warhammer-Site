Aftermath and Legacy

The Emperor's Sacrifice

Mortally wounded in his battle with Horus, the Emperor was interred in the Golden Throne, where he remains for over 10,000 years.

Legion Reforms

The loyal Space Marine Legions were broken down into smaller Chapters to prevent any single commander from wielding too much power.

Rise of the Ecclesiarchy

The Emperor's godhood was proclaimed, leading to the establishment of the Imperial Cult as the state religion.

Age of Darkness

The Imperium entered a long period of technological and cultural regression known as the Age of Darkness.
