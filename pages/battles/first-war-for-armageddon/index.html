The War

The First War for Armageddon began when the Daemon Primarch Angron led his World Eaters Legion in a massive assault on the vital Industrial World of Armageddon.

The Invasion

Angron's forces quickly overwhelmed many of Armageddon's defenses, with the World Eaters and Khorne Daemons wreaking havoc across the planet. The situation seemed dire until the arrival of the Space Wolves under Logan Grimnar.

The Counter-Attack

The combined forces of the Space Wolves and Grey Knights launched a desperate counter-offensive. While the Space Wolves engaged the World Eaters, the Grey Knights confronted Angron himself in a battle that would determine the fate of Armageddon.

Aftermath

Though the Imperium emerged victorious, the cost was devastating. Large portions of Armageddon lay in ruins, and the civilian population had suffered horrific casualties.

Major Consequences

  • Massive civilian casualties
  • Widespread destruction of infrastructure
  • Implementation of strict security measures
  • Beginning of Armageddon's militarization
