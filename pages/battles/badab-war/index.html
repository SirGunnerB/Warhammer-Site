The War

The Badab War was a major rebellion against Imperial authority, primarily fought between Space Marine Chapters. It began when Lufgt Huron, Chapter Master of the Astral Claws, declared independence from the Imperium.

The Secession

The conflict started when Huron refused to submit to Imperial authority and taxation, declaring the Badab Sector independent. Several Space Marine Chapters joined his cause, either through manipulation or shared grievances with Imperial bureaucracy.

The Imperial Response

The Imperium responded by sending multiple Space Marine Chapters and Navy battlegroups to quell the rebellion. The war escalated into a series of brutal void battles and planetary assaults, culminating in the siege of Badab Primaris.

Aftermath

The Badab War resulted in significant changes to the balance of power in the Maelstrom Zone and served as a warning about the potential dangers of Space Marine autonomy.

Major Consequences

  • Destruction of Badab Primaris
  • Huron's transformation into Huron Blackheart
  • Penitent Crusades for surviving Secessionist Chapters
  • Reorganization of the Maelstrom Zone
