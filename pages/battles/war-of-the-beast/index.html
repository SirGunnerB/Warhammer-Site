The War

The War of the Beast was the greatest Ork WAAAGH! since the time of the Great Crusade, led by an unprecedented number of Beast-class Orks who possessed technology far beyond typical Ork capabilities.

The Rise of the Beast

The conflict began when massive Ork Attack Moons appeared throughout Imperial space, demonstrating technological capabilities that shocked the Imperium. These space-faring fortresses could teleport across vast distances and devastate entire systems.

The Imperial Response

The Imperium's response was hampered by political infighting among the High Lords of Terra. The situation became so dire that Terra itself came under direct attack, leading to the most significant mobilization of Imperial forces since the Horus Heresy.

Aftermath

The War of the Beast reshaped Imperial military doctrine and led to significant political reforms within the Imperium. The near-total destruction of the Imperial Fists and subsequent refounding would influence Chapter organization for millennia to come.

Major Consequences

  • Reformation of the High Lords of Terra
  • Creation of the Deathwatch
  • Rebuilding of the Imperial Fists
  • Implementation of new defense protocols
