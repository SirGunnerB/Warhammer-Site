The Crusade

The Indomitus Crusade represents the Imperium's largest military campaign since the Great Crusade, aimed at reclaiming the dark half of the Imperium after the formation of the Great Rift.

The Beginning

Led by the resurrected Primarch Roboute Guilliman, the crusade launched with the unveiling of the Primaris Space Marines, representing a new generation of transhuman warriors. The crusade's initial push focused on securing vital supply lines and strategic positions.

The Campaign

Over the course of several decades, the crusade fought countless battles across thousands of worlds. Guilliman's strategic genius, combined with the power of the Primaris Marines and the fervor of the faithful, allowed the Imperium to reclaim vast swathes of territory.

Aftermath

While the crusade continues in various forms, its initial phase has reshaped the balance of power in the Imperium Nihilus and demonstrated that humanity can still strike back against its enemies.

Major Consequences

  • Integration of Primaris Marines
  • Reclamation of lost territories
  • Reorganization of Imperial forces
  • Establishment of new supply lines
