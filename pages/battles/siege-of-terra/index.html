The Siege

The Siege of Terra marked the culmination of the Horus Heresy, a devastating civil war that tore the Imperium apart. For 55 days, the forces of the Warmaster Horus laid siege to the Imperial Palace, while the Emperor's loyal forces, led by Rogal Dorn, mounted a desperate defense.

The Defense of the Palace

Rogal Dorn had spent years fortifying the Imperial Palace, turning it into an almost impregnable fortress. The outer walls stood hundreds of meters high, bristling with defense batteries and void shields. Despite this, the overwhelming forces of Chaos gradually pushed through the defenses, yard by bloody yard.

The Final Battle

With loyalist reinforcements approaching Terra, Horus made the fateful decision to lower his flagship's void shields, inviting the Emperor to teleport aboard for a final confrontation. The Emperor, accompanied by Sanguinius and a contingent of warriors, accepted the challenge, leading to the climactic duel that would determine humanity's fate.

Aftermath

The Siege of Terra ended with the death of Horus and the near-death of the Emperor, who was interred upon the Golden Throne. The surviving traitor forces fled into the Eye of Terror, while the Imperium began its long process of rebuilding, entering the dark age known as the Time of Ending.

Major Consequences

  • The Emperor interred on the Golden Throne
  • Beginning of the Imperial Cult
  • Reformation of the Imperium under Roboute Guilliman
  • Creation of the Codex Astartes
