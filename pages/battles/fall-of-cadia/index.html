The Battle

The Fall of Cadia marked the successful conclusion of Abaddon's 13th Black Crusade and the destruction of one of the Imperium's most strategically important worlds.

The Defense

The Cadian defense was led by the legendary Cadian Shock Troops, supported by multiple Space Marine chapters and the unexpected arrival of Saint Celestine. The defenders fought with unprecedented determination, knowing the fate of the Imperium hung in the balance.

The Final Blow

When conventional warfare failed to break Cadia's defense, Abaddon resorted to crashing the damaged Blackstone Fortress Will of Eternity into the planet's surface, destabilizing its core and ultimately leading to its destruction.

Aftermath

The destruction of Cadia led to the formation of the Great Rift, forever changing the face of the galaxy. The loss of this strategic fortress world created a vital breach in Imperial defenses.

Major Consequences

  • Formation of the Great Rift
  • Loss of the Cadian Gate
  • Beginning of the Dark Imperium
  • Awakening of Roboute Guilliman
