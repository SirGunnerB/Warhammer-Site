The Massacre

The Drop Site Massacre was one of the most devastating betrayals in Imperial history, where four supposedly loyal Space Marine Legions revealed their true allegiance to Horus by turning on their brothers at a crucial moment in battle.

The First Wave

The Iron Hands, Salamanders, and Raven Guard made planetfall on Istvaan V to confront the forces of Horus. They believed they would be supported by seven other Space Marine Legions in a second wave, but this would prove to be their undoing.

The Betrayal

When the second wave arrived, instead of supporting their loyal brothers, they revealed their true allegiance to Horus and opened fire on the loyalist forces. Caught between the anvil of Horus's forces and the hammer of the traitor legions, the loyal forces were almost completely annihilated.

Aftermath

The Drop Site Massacre effectively destroyed three entire Space Marine Legions in a single day, dealing a devastating blow to the Emperor's forces at the outset of the Horus Heresy.

Major Consequences

  • Death of Ferrus Manus
  • Near destruction of three loyal legions
  • Significant boost to Horus's forces
  • Permanent scarring of the surviving loyalists
