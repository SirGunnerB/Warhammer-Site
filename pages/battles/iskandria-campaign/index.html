The Iskandria Campaign

War for the Crown World (784.M41)

Overview

The Iskandria Campaign was a major conflict fought over the Crown World of Iskandria, a vital strategic location in the Sabbat Worlds Crusade. The campaign saw Imperial forces battling against both Chaos forces and Necron awakening, making it a complex three-way war for control of the planet.

Imperial Forces

  • Volpone Bluebloods
  • Roane Deepers
  • Adeptus Mechanicus Forces
  • Various PDF Forces

Enemy Forces

Necron Forces

  • Awakened Necron Dynasty
  • Necron Constructs
  • Canoptek Constructs

Major Events

Initial Invasion

The campaign began with Chaos forces launching a surprise attack on the Crown World, catching the Imperial defenders off guard and establishing multiple strongholds across the planet.

Necron Awakening

During the midst of the Imperial counter-offensive, ancient Necron tombs began to activate, adding a third faction to the already complex battlefield situation.

The Three-Way War

The campaign evolved into a complex three-way conflict, with each faction fighting against the other two while trying to secure strategic objectives across the planet.

Strategic Significance

Iskandria's position as a Crown World made it a vital strategic asset in the Sabbat Worlds Crusade. Its loss would have created a significant gap in Imperial defenses and provided the forces of Chaos with a staging ground for further incursions into Imperial space. The awakening of the Necron presence added another layer of complexity to the strategic importance of the world.

Aftermath and Consequences

  • Significant damage to planetary infrastructure
  • Discovery of extensive Necron tomb complexes
  • Establishment of permanent Mechanicus research facilities
  • Strengthening of planetary defenses
  • Reorganization of local Imperial Guard forces
  • Implementation of new anti-Necron protocols
