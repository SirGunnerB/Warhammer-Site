The Great Crusade

Humanity's Greatest Campaign of Expansion (Late M30)

Overview

The Great Crusade was a monumental 200-year campaign led by the Emperor of Mankind to reclaim and reunite the human-inhabited worlds of the galaxy under the banner of the Imperium of Man. This massive undertaking marked humanity's resurgence after the Age of Strife and laid the foundation for the Imperium as we know it.

Key Participants

Imperial Forces

  • The Emperor of Mankind
  • All Twenty Space Marine Legions
  • Imperial Army (Later Imperial Guard)
  • Mechanicum Forces
  • Legio Custodes
  • Sisters of Silence

Opposition Forces

  • Xenos Empires
  • Human Separatist Kingdoms
  • Technobarbarians
  • Alien-Human Hybrid Civilizations
  • Various Independent Human Worlds

Major Events

Unification of Terra

The Emperor's initial campaign to unite Terra under his rule, defeating the various techno-barbarian states and establishing the foundation of the Imperium.

Treaty of Mars

The formal alliance between the Emperor and the Mechanicum of Mars, providing the Imperium with the technological base needed for the Great Crusade.

Discovery of the Primarchs

Throughout the Great Crusade, the Emperor discovered his scattered sons, the Primarchs, each leading their own worlds or armies, and reunited them with their respective Space Marine Legions.

Key Locations

  • Terra - The birthplace of humanity
  • Mars - Forge World of the Mechanicum
  • Ullanor - Site of the greatest triumph
  • Rangda Xenocides - Major xenos war zones
  • Various Expedition Fleets' routes

Strategic Significance

The Great Crusade established the Imperium of Man as the dominant power in the galaxy, uniting countless human worlds under one banner and establishing the infrastructure that would allow the Imperium to survive for millennia to come. It also led to the creation and refinement of many technologies and military strategies still in use in the 41st Millennium.

Aftermath and Consequences

  • Creation of the Imperium of Man
  • Establishment of the Imperial Truth
  • Formation of the Space Marine Legions
  • Alliance with the Mechanicum
  • Eradication of many xenos species
  • Set the stage for the Horus Heresy
