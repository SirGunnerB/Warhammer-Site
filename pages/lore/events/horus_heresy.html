The Horus Heresy

M31 Major Historical Event

The Horus Heresy was the pivotal moment in human history that shaped the grim darkness of the far future. This devastating civil war tore the Emperor's dream of unity asunder and transformed the Imperium forever.

203.M31 - Triumph of Ullanor

The Emperor names Horus as Warmaster and returns to Terra, marking the beginning of growing discontent among some Primarchs.

205.M31 - Corruption at Davin

Horus is mortally wounded and healed in a Chaos temple on Davin's moon, where the Chaos Gods show him corrupted visions of the future.

206.M31 - Isstvan III Atrocity

Horus begins his rebellion by virus bombing loyalist elements within the traitor legions on Isstvan III.

206.M31 - Drop Site Massacre

Seven loyal legions arrive at Isstvan V to confront Horus. Four reveal their treachery, nearly destroying three loyal legions.

207.M31 - Schism of Mars

The Mechanicum splits into loyal and traitor factions, beginning a civil war on Mars that deprives both sides of crucial war materiel.

209.M31 - Siege of Terra Begins

Horus's forces lay siege to Terra itself, beginning the final phase of the Heresy.

211.M31 - Final Battle

The Emperor teleports aboard Horus's flagship for their final confrontation. Though victorious, the Emperor is mortally wounded.

211.M31 - Golden Throne

The Emperor is interred in the Golden Throne, beginning His ten-thousand-year vigil. The Traitor Legions flee to the Eye of Terror.

Origins of Betrayal

Following the success of the Great Crusade, the Emperor returned to Terra to work on the secret Webway Project, leaving Horus Lupercal, his most trusted son, as Warmaster to lead the continuing expansion of the Imperium. This fateful decision, combined with growing dissatisfaction among some Primarchs, set the stage for the greatest betrayal in human history.

The Corruption of Horus

On the moon of Davin, Horus was mortally wounded and healed in a corrupted temple. During this time, the Chaos Gods showed him visions of a future where the Emperor was worshipped as a god - everything he and his father had fought against. These visions, though twisted by Chaos, convinced Horus that the Emperor had betrayed his own ideals.

The Civil War

The war began with the virus bombing of Isstvan III, where Horus purged loyal elements from the traitor legions. This was followed by the Drop Site Massacre on Isstvan V, where three loyal legions were almost completely destroyed by their betraying brothers. The galaxy burned as brother fought brother, with Space Marine fighting Space Marine and the Mechanicum tearing itself apart in the Schism of Mars.

The Siege of Terra

The culmination of the Heresy came with the Siege of Terra, where Horus's forces laid siege to the Imperial Palace itself. In the final battle aboard Horus's flagship, the Vengeful Spirit, the Emperor confronted his fallen son. Though the Emperor emerged victorious, he was mortally wounded and has remained interred in the Golden Throne for ten thousand years.

Legacy

The aftermath of the Heresy transformed the Imperium into the authoritarian, religious state it is in the 41st Millennium. The Emperor's dream of a secular human empire was replaced by the Imperial Cult, and the Traitor Legions were driven into the Eye of Terror, from where they continue to wage the Long War against the Imperium.
