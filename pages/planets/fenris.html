Overview

Fenris is the harsh and brutal home world of the Space Wolves Chapter of the Adeptus Astartes. A death world of ice, fire, and monster-filled oceans, it forges some of the strongest warriors in the Imperium through its unforgiving environment.

History

Pre-Imperial Era

Ancient settlement of Fenris by human colonists, who regressed to a primitive state during the Age of Strife.

Great Crusade (Late M30)

Discovery of Leman Russ by the Emperor. Establishment of the VI Legion's base on Fenris.

Horus Heresy (M31)

Space Wolves remained loyal to the Emperor. Burning of Prospero and the first battle between Leman Russ and Magnus the Red.

The First Battle of The Fang (M32)

Magnus the Red's first attempt to destroy the Space Wolves' fortress-monastery, ultimately repelled.

The Month of Shame (M32)

Conflict between the Space Wolves and the Inquisition over the fate of survivors from the First War of Armageddon.

The Battle of Midgardia (M36)

Space Wolves defend their protectorate world against a massive Thousand Sons assault.

The Battle for the Fenris System (M41)

Magnus the Red returns with a massive Thousand Sons fleet, nearly destroying The Fang before being repelled by the Space Wolves and their allies.

Siege of Fenris (M41)

Chaos forces led by Magnus the Red launch another assault. The Changeling infiltrates The Fang, but is ultimately defeated.

War Zone Fenris (Late M41)

Massive daemonic invasion of the Fenris System, requiring intervention from multiple Space Marine Chapters and the Grey Knights.

Era Indomitus (M42)

Fenris endures the formation of the Great Rift. Space Wolves adapt to incorporate Primaris Marines while maintaining their unique culture.

Geography & Climate

Environmental Features

  • Constantly shifting continents due to tectonic activity
  • Massive oceans filled with legendary sea monsters
  • Volcanic regions that regularly reshape the landscape
  • Year-round freezing temperatures except near volcanic areas

Notable Regions

  • Asaheim - The only stable continent
  • The Kraken's Deep - Most dangerous of Fenris's oceans
  • The Mountains of the Maelstrom - Perpetually storm-wracked peaks
  • The Fire Breather - Largest active volcano chain

Society & Culture

Tribal Structure

Fenrisian society is organized into various warrior tribes that constantly compete for resources and glory. These tribes provide the recruitment pool for the Space Wolves Chapter.

Warrior Traditions

  • Trial of Morkai - Ritual challenge for Space Wolf aspirants
  • Sagas - Oral traditions recording great deeds
  • Great Feasts - Celebrations of victories and fallen warriors

Daily Life

Life on Fenris is a constant struggle for survival against both the elements and the planet's deadly fauna. Tribes must be nomadic due to the constantly changing landscape.

The Fang

Architecture

The largest fortress-monastery in the Imperium, The Fang rises over 20,000 feet from the surface of Asaheim. It contains:

  • Massive void shields and defense systems
  • Training grounds and feasting halls
  • Ancient technologies from the Dark Age of Technology
  • Chambers of the Living Stone - Where aspirants become Space Wolves

Strategic Importance

The Fang serves as both the Space Wolves' fortress-monastery and one of the most formidable defensive structures in the Imperium, protecting the northern regions of the galaxy.
