Macragge

Chapter World Active Imperium

Macragge is the capital world of Ultramar and the homeworld of the Ultramarines Space Marine Chapter. It is a place of both martial excellence and civil virtue, where the traditions of the XIII Legion and its Primarch, Roboute Guilliman, are preserved with reverence.

Geography and Climate

Macragge is a world of varied climate and geography, featuring towering mountain ranges, vast polar ice caps, and temperate regions suitable for agriculture. The planet's most notable features are the Fortress of Hera, seat of the Ultramarines, and the city of Magna Macragge Civitas, the planetary capital.

Society and Culture

The society of Macragge is highly organized and efficient, reflecting the principles laid down by Roboute Guilliman. The population is well-educated and disciplined, with a strong emphasis on civic duty and martial prowess. The planet serves as both a military fortress and a model of Imperial governance.

Notable Locations

  • Fortress of Hera

    The mighty fortress-monastery of the Ultramarines, containing the Chapter's primary training facilities, armories, and sacred halls.

  • Temple of Correction

    Where the preserved body of Roboute Guilliman lay in stasis for millennia before his resurrection in M42.

  • Magna Macragge Civitas

    The capital city, exemplifying the perfect balance between military efficiency and civilian prosperity.

Military Significance

As the homeworld of the Ultramarines, Macragge is one of the most heavily defended planets in the Imperium. It has withstood numerous invasions, most notably the First Tyrannic War, where the Ultramarines successfully defended their home against Hive Fleet Behemoth.

Historical Events

Late M30 - Arrival of Guilliman

Roboute Guilliman lands on Macragge and quickly rises to become its ruler through his tactical and administrative genius.

M31 - Foundation of Ultramar

Macragge becomes the capital of the growing realm of Ultramar under Guilliman's leadership.

745.M41 - First Tyrannic War

Macragge successfully repels the invasion of Hive Fleet Behemoth, though at great cost to the Ultramarines.

999.M41 - Resurrection

Roboute Guilliman is restored to life in the Temple of Correction, beginning a new chapter in Macragge's history.
