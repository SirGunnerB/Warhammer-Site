Overview

Cadia stood as the Imperium's most important fortress world, guarding the only stable path through the Cadian Gate from the Eye of Terror. Its destruction during Abaddon's 13th Black Crusade marked one of the darkest moments in Imperial history.

History

Pre-Imperial Era

Ancient Eldar civilization built pylons across Cadia to contain Warp storms. These pylons would later prove crucial in containing the Eye of Terror.

Great Crusade (M30)

Imperial forces discovered and colonized Cadia, recognizing its strategic importance near the Eye of Terror.

Horus Heresy (M31)

Cadia served as a staging ground for Chaos forces during their push toward Terra. The population was corrupted and later purged.

First Black Crusade (781.M31)

Abaddon's first attempt to capture Cadia, establishing a pattern that would continue for millennia.

Eye of Night (Unknown Date.M32)

Mysterious artifact discovered in Cadian orbit, later stolen by Chaos forces.

Gothic War (139-160.M41)

Cadia served as a crucial supply base during the 12th Black Crusade.

13th Black Crusade (999.M41)

Final destruction of Cadia by Abaddon's forces, using the Blackstone Fortress as a planet-killer weapon.

Military Strength

Cadian Shock Troops

The legendary Cadian military was considered the finest of all Imperial Guard regiments, with every citizen trained from birth in military disciplines.

Standing Forces

  • Multiple Cadian Shock Troop Regiments
  • Kasrkin elite special forces
  • Extensive PDF forces
  • Titan Legion support
  • Naval defense fleets

Defense Network

  • Orbital defense platforms
  • Surface-to-orbit weapon batteries
  • Void shield networks
  • Underground bunker complexes

Society & Culture

Military Life

Every aspect of Cadian society was geared toward military service. Children learned to field-strip a lasgun before they could read, and military service was universal.

The Cadian Way

  • Universal military training from childhood
  • Strong emphasis on discipline and duty
  • Proud martial traditions
  • Deep hatred of Chaos

Notable Traditions

  • The Whiteshield youth program
  • Interior Guard rotation
  • Battle shrine pilgrimages
  • Victory Day celebrations

The Fall of Cadia

Final Battle

The 13th Black Crusade culminated in the destruction of Cadia when Abaddon crashed the remains of the Blackstone Fortress into the planet's surface, destabilizing it completely.

Key Events

  • Destruction of the Pylons
  • Breaking of the Cadian Gate
  • Mass evacuation attempts
  • Formation of the Great Rift

Legacy

"Cadia Stands" became a rallying cry for Imperial forces across the galaxy, symbolizing defiance in the face of overwhelming odds. The surviving Cadians continue to fight across the Imperium, maintaining their traditions and training new generations of soldiers.
