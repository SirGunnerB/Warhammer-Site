Overview

Commorragh is not a planet in the conventional sense, but rather a massive collection of sub-realms and satellite realms within the Webway. It is the dark heart of the Drukhari empire and the largest port city in existence, though it exists entirely within the labyrinthine dimension of the Webway.

History

The Fall of the Eldar

As the Aeldari empire descended into hedonism, the port of Commorragh became a haven for the most depraved elements of their society.

Birth of Slaanesh

When Slaanesh was born, Commorragh survived within the Webway, protected from the destruction that claimed the Aeldari empire.

Rise of Asdrubael Vect

Vect manipulated events to destroy the noble houses and establish the Kabal system, becoming the Supreme Overlord of Commorragh.

Destruction of the Obsidian Gate

Major gateway sealed to prevent daemon incursion, reshaping Commorragh's internal geography.

Dysjunction

Massive reality-breach caused by Yvraine's awakening that led to widespread destruction and reconstruction.

Structure & Layout

Major Districts

  • High Commorragh - Realm of the noble houses and most powerful Kabals
  • Low Commorragh - Sprawling undercity of lesser Kabals and slaves
  • Port Carmine - Major trading hub for raiders
  • Null City - Mysterious region where realspace laws don't apply

Notable Locations

  • The Black Heart - Vect's personal domain
  • Crucibael Arena - Largest gladiatorial arena
  • The Sprawls - Endless maze of lesser districts
  • The Dark Gates - Major Webway portals

Society & Culture

Social Structure

Commorragh's society is built on a foundation of slavery, torture, and constant political intrigue. Power is held by the Kabals, with the Haemonculus Covens and Wych Cults forming other major power blocks.

Daily Life

  • Constant struggle for survival and power
  • Raids into realspace for slaves and resources
  • Elaborate political machinations and betrayals
  • Gladiatorial entertainment in the arenas

Economy

Based on slavery, stolen technology, and the trading of souls and exotic materials. The Dark City operates as a massive black market for the galaxy's most forbidden goods.

Power Structure

Major Power Groups

  • Kabals - Political and military organizations
  • Wych Cults - Gladiatorial organizations
  • Haemonculus Covens - Flesh-crafters and scientists
  • Incubi Temples - Warrior elite

Notable Kabals

  • Kabal of the Black Heart - Ruled by Asdrubael Vect
  • Kabal of the Poisoned Tongue - Led by Lady Malys
  • Kabal of the Obsidian Rose - Masters of weaponcrafting

Notable Events

Vect's Ascension

Complex plot involving a Space Marine Chapter that led to the destruction of the noble houses.

The Imperium Breaches

Several Imperial invasions of Commorragh, including notable Space Marine raids.

Birth of Yvraine

Catastrophic event that caused massive damage to several districts.

The Three Days of Blood

Major uprising that reshaped the power structure of several districts.

Khaine's Gate Incident

Daemon incursion that threatened the entire Dark City.
