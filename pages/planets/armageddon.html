Overview

Armageddon is a vital Imperial Hive World in the Segmentum Solar, known for its massive industrial output and as the site of several major wars. Its strategic importance and vast manufacturing capabilities make it one of the most crucial worlds in the Imperium.

History

Pre-Imperial Era (Ullanor)

Originally known as Ullanor, the planet was the capital of the largest Ork empire ever encountered by the Imperium.

Great Crusade

Site of the Ullanor Crusade, where the Emperor of Mankind and Horus defeated the Ork Empire. The Emperor later declared Horus as Warmaster here.

Relocation

After the Horus Heresy, the Mechanicum relocated the entire planet to its current position, renaming it Armageddon.

First War for Armageddon (444.M41)

Angron and the World Eaters invaded, defeated by the Space Wolves and Grey Knights.

Second War for Armageddon (941-943.M41)

Massive Ork WAAAGH! led by Ghazghkull Thraka, defended by Commander Yarrick.

Third War for Armageddon (998.M41)

Return of Ghazghkull with an even larger WAAAGH!, resulting in ongoing conflict.

Geography

Terrain Features

  • Vast ash wastes
  • Equatorial jungle
  • Polluted seas
  • Volcanic regions

Climate

Armageddon's climate is harsh and unforgiving, with toxic ash storms and extreme temperature variations. The equatorial regions maintain a jungle environment, while the poles are perpetually frozen.

Wars of Armageddon

First War

  • Chaos invasion led by Angron
  • Grey Knights banish Angron
  • Inquisitorial purge of civilian population

Second War

  • Ghazghkull's first invasion
  • Rise of Commissar Yarrick
  • Massive hive city battles
  • Imperial victory through attrition

Third War

  • Largest Ork invasion in history
  • Multiple Space Marine Chapters involved
  • Ongoing guerrilla warfare
  • Establishment of permanent war footing

Industry

Manufacturing Output

  • Weapons and ammunition
  • Vehicle production

  • Mining operations

Resources

Armageddon's vast industrial complexes produce everything from basic ammunition to Titan components. The planet's rich mineral deposits and extensive refineries make it one of the Imperium's most valuable manufacturing worlds.
