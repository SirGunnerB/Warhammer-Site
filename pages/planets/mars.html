Overview

Mars, also known as the Red Planet, is the primary Forge World of the Adeptus Mechanicus and one of the most important planets in the Imperium of Man. It forms one half of the twin capitals of the Imperium along with Terra, and is the headquarters of the Adeptus Mechanicus.

History

Age of Terra

During the Age of Terra, Mars was one of humanity's first colonized planets, becoming a center of technological and scientific advancement. The first colonists established the foundations of what would become the Mechanicum.

Dark Age of Technology

Mars reached its technological zenith, developing advanced AI, nanotechnology, and other wonders. The planet was transformed into a technological paradise.

Age of Strife

Mars survived the Age of Strife through the preservation of technology by the Mechanicum. The rise of the Cult Mechanicus occurred during this time, as technology became viewed as divine. Many forge-temples were established.

Schism of Mars (M23)

Civil war erupted between those who embraced AI and those who feared it, leading to the establishment of strict doctrines against "Abominable Intelligence."

Treaty of Mars (ca. M30)

The Emperor of Mankind and the Fabricator-General of Mars signed the Treaty of Mars, formally joining Mars to the Imperium while maintaining its autonomy. Mars became the primary supplier of weapons and technology for the Great Crusade.

Horus Heresy (M31)

Mars was torn apart by civil war between loyal and traitor Mechanicum forces. The Dark Mechanicum emerged, and many of Mars's greatest secrets were lost or corrupted.

The Moirae Schism (M35)

A major doctrinal dispute led to conflict between different Mechanicus factions, affecting forge worlds across the Imperium.

The Martian Civil War (M41)

Internal conflict erupted over interpretation of ancient texts and the discovery of new STCs, leading to warfare between different forge-temples.

Invasion of Mars (M41)

During the 13th Black Crusade, Chaos forces attempted to corrupt Mars's forge-temples, but were repelled by the combined forces of the Mechanicus.

Era Indomitus (M42)

Mars plays a crucial role in the development of Primaris Space Marine technology and the modernization of Imperial forces following the formation of the Great Rift.

Society & Culture

Social Structure

Mars is governed by the Fabricator-General and the Parliament of Mars. Society is strictly hierarchical, based on technological knowledge and augmentation level.

Religious Beliefs

The Cult Mechanicus dominates all aspects of Martian society, with worship of the Machine God (Omnissiah) being the state religion.

Daily Life

Life on Mars revolves around the endless cycle of production and maintenance of technology for the Imperium. Most citizens are heavily augmented with cybernetic enhancements.
