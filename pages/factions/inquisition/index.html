The Holy Orders of the Emperor's Inquisition

None Shall Escape Our Vigilance

Overview

The Inquisition is the secret police force of the Imperium, tasked with investigating, judging, and eliminating threats to humanity. Operating with nearly unlimited authority, Inquisitors are among the most feared and respected individuals in the galaxy.

Each Inquisitor operates independently, with the power to requisition any Imperial resource and the authority to condemn entire worlds. They are divided into different Ordos, each focusing on specific threats to the Imperium.

Founded in the aftermath of the Horus Heresy by Malcador the Sigillite, the Inquisition serves as the Emperor's eyes and ears across the galaxy. Their authority comes directly from the Golden Throne, making them answerable only to the Emperor himself.

While independent in their operations, Inquisitors often form networks and cabals to share information and resources. These alliances are temporary and fluid, reflecting the pragmatic nature of their work.

Major Ordos

Ordo Hereticus

The Witch Hunters, who combat internal threats to the Imperium:

  • Heretics and Rebels - Including religious cults and political dissidents
  • Rogue Psykers - Unregistered psychics who pose a threat to Imperial stability
  • Mutants - Those whose genetic deviation threatens humanity's purity
  • Corrupt Imperial Officials - Including planetary governors and military leaders
  • Maintains close ties with the Ecclesiarchy and Adepta Sororitas

Ordo Xenos

The Alien Hunters, who protect humanity from xenos threats:

  • Alien Infiltration - Including shapeshifters and mind-controllers
  • Xenos Technology - Study and containment of alien artifacts
  • Genestealer Cults - Early detection and elimination of hybrid infections
  • Human-Xenos Collaboration - Tracking and eliminating xenophile networks
  • Works closely with the Deathwatch Space Marines

Ordo Malleus

The Daemon Hunters, who combat the forces of Chaos:

  • Daemonic Incursions - Preventing and containing warp breaches
  • Chaos Cults - Infiltrating and destroying chaos worship groups
  • Warp Phenomena - Investigation of anomalous psychic activity
  • Chaos Artifacts - Securing and destroying corrupted relics
  • Commands the Grey Knights Space Marine Chapter

Notable Inquisitors

Gregor Eisenhorn

Famous Ordo Xenos Inquisitor who gradually became more radical. Known for the Eisenhorn Trilogy, his journey from puritan to radical exemplifies the complex moral choices Inquisitors face. Successfully hunted the heretic Pontius Glaw and prevented multiple catastrophic threats to the Imperium.

Fyodor Karamazov

Notorious Puritan of the Ordo Hereticus, known as the Pyrophant Judge. Rides the Throne of Judgment, a massive walking cathedral. Famous for saying "There is no such thing as innocence, only degrees of guilt." Has ordered the execution of several planetary governors.

Torquemada Coteaz

Powerful Ordo Malleus Inquisitor known for his prophetic abilities and his familiar, a cyber-eagle named Malicant. Has uncovered numerous daemonic plots and maintains a vast network of spies and informants across multiple sectors.

Amberley Vail

Ordo Xenos Inquisitor known for her subtle approach and manipulation of events from behind the scenes. Notable for her collaboration with Commissar Ciaphas Cain and her extensive documentation of his exploits.

Notable Operations

The Months of Shame

A dark period following the First War for Armageddon, where the Inquisition attempted to contain knowledge of Chaos by purging Imperial Guard regiments and civilian populations who had witnessed the Grey Knights in action.

The Plague of Unbelief

Joint operation between Ordo Hereticus and Ordo Malleus to root out a massive Chaos cult that had infiltrated multiple levels of planetary government across an entire sub-sector.

The Orphean War

Massive containment operation by Ordo Xenos following the discovery of awakening Necron Tomb Worlds in the Orpheus Sector, culminating in several Exterminatus actions.
