Adeptus Mechanicus

Praise the Omnissiah

Overview

The Adeptus Mechanicus is the technological and scientific organization of the Imperium. Based on Mars, they are the guardians of technology, maintaining ancient machinery and searching for lost knowledge. Their members believe in the Machine God (Omnissiah) and see the preservation and recovery of technology as a religious duty.

Through the Treaty of Mars (also known as the Treaty of Olympus Mons), they maintain significant autonomy within the Imperium while providing crucial technical expertise, manufacturing capabilities, and military support to humanity's empire. This ancient pact, signed during the Great Crusade, ensures the Mechanicus' independence in exchange for their loyalty to the Emperor.

The Tech-Priests of the Mechanicus seek to replace their organic components with mechanical parts, viewing flesh as weak and technology as divine. This process of augmentation is both practical and religious, as they believe it brings them closer to their Machine God.

Religious Beliefs

The Omnissiah

The Machine God, believed to be an aspect of the Emperor, who represents the perfect union of technology and divinity. The Mechanicus believes all technology contains a fraction of the Machine God's power.

  • Universal Machine Spirit
  • Divine Knowledge
  • Perfect Logic

The Motive Force

The energy that powers all machinery and technology, seen as the breath of the Omnissiah. It represents the divine energy flowing through all machines.

  • Divine Energy
  • Life Force of Machines
  • Technical Animation
