Eldar

Ancient Race of Sophistication - Masters of the Webway

Overview

The Eldar are an ancient race of highly sophisticated humanoids, once rulers of a vast galactic empire. Now divided into several distinct cultures after their civilization's fall, they fight to survive in a hostile universe while carrying the burden of their past.

  • Origin: Old Ones' Creation
  • Current Status: Scattered Civilizations
  • Government: Craftworld Councils
  • Military Doctrine: Precision Strikes
