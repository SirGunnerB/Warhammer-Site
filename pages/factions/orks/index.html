Orks

The Green Tide - Da Biggest and da Baddest!

Overview

The Orks are a savage, warlike race that spreads across the galaxy like a green tide. They live for combat and believe that might makes right, creating a society where the strongest rule and the weak serve. Their technology, while appearing crude, functions through a mysterious psychic field known as the "WAAAGH!"

  • Origin: Bio-engineered by the Old Ones
  • Current Status: Active across the galaxy
  • Organization: Tribal/Clan-based
  • Military Doctrine: Overwhelming Force/WAAAGH!
