Adeptus Custodes

The Emperor's Personal Guard

Overview

The Adeptus Custodes are the Emperor's personal bodyguards and companions, created using ancient technology and secrets known only to the Emperor himself. Each Custodian is individually crafted through a complex process that transforms them at a cellular level, making them superior to even Space Marines in both physical capability and tactical acumen.

Unlike Space Marines, who are created in bulk through gene-seed implantation, each Custodian is a unique masterpiece, crafted through arcane processes that begin in early childhood. Their creation process is so intricate and personalized that no two Custodians are exactly alike in their capabilities or appearance.

Following Guilliman's return and the formation of the Primaris Space Marines, the Custodes have taken a more active role beyond Terra's walls, leading crusades and defending Imperial interests across the galaxy while maintaining their primary duty of protecting the Golden Throne.

Organization Statistics

  • Headquarters: The Imperial Palace, Terra
  • Founded: Late Age of Strife/Unification Wars
  • Leader: Captain-General Trajann Valoris
  • Strength: Ten Thousand (The Legio Custodes)
  • Status: Active
  • Authority: Direct from the Emperor
  • Notable Campaigns: Unification Wars, Webway War, War of the Beast
  • Creation Time: Decades per warrior

Organization

Shield Hosts

Primary combat formations of the Custodes, each dedicated to specific duties and traditions.

  • Solar Watch - Patrol and defend the Sol System
  • Shadowkeepers - Guard ancient threats beneath the Imperial Palace
  • Dread Host - Spearhead offensive operations
  • Aquilan Shield - Protect key Imperial figures
  • Emissaries Imperatus - Carry out diplomatic missions
  • Gilded Talons - Specialized reconnaissance operations
  • Paladin Guard - Elite palace defenders

Ranks and Titles

  • Captain-General - Supreme commander of the Custodian Guard
  • Tribune - High-ranking officers and advisors
  • Shield-Captain - Field commanders of Custodian forces
  • Vexilus Praetor - Standard bearers and battle coordinators
  • Custodian Guard - Standard battle-brothers
  • Eyes of the Emperor - Retired Custodians serving as spies

Combat Roles

Custodian Guard

Standard Custodians equipped with guardian spears or sentinel blades and storm shields. Masters of both ranged and melee combat.

  • Guardian Spear - Combination power weapon and bolt weapon
  • Sentinel Blade - Power sword with built-in bolt caster
  • Tactical flexibility in all combat situations

Allarus Terminators

Elite warriors in Allarus Terminator armor for special operations and decapitation strikes.

  • Castellan Axe - Heavy power weapon
  • Balistus Grenade Launcher
  • Superior tactical dexterity despite heavy armor

Vertus Praetors

Jetbike-mounted rapid response units, masters of aerial combat.

  • Dawneagle Jetbikes - Ancient anti-grav vehicles
  • Hurricane Bolters or Salvo Launchers
  • Unmatched mobility and striking power

Wardens

Elite bodyguards of the Captain-General and masters of the Custodian fighting arts.

  • Castellan Axe specialists
  • Enhanced tactical coordination
  • Veterans of countless battles

Traditions and Duties

Blood Games

Regular exercises where Custodians attempt to infiltrate the Imperial Palace to test its defenses and their own skills.

Vigil Eternal

The never-ending watch over the Emperor's mortal form, maintained without pause since the Horus Heresy.

Names and Titles

Each Custodian earns names and titles through their deeds, sometimes accumulating dozens throughout their service.

The Records of Unity

Ancient texts maintained by the Custodes chronicling their history and the Emperor's teachings.
