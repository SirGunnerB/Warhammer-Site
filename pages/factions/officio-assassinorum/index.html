Officio Assassinorum

Death in the Shadows

Overview

The Officio Assassinorum is the Imperial organization responsible for training and deploying elite assassins. Operating from their hidden temples on Terra, they eliminate threats to the Imperium with surgical precision, whether those threats are political, military, or spiritual in nature.

Each temple specializes in a different method of assassination, creating operatives of unparalleled skill in their chosen art of death. The deployment of an Imperial Assassin requires the highest level of authorization, typically from the High Lords of Terra themselves.

Founded during the Great Crusade by Malcador the Sigillite, the Officio Assassinorum has served as the Emperor's hidden blade for over ten millennia. Their existence is known to few outside the highest echelons of Imperial power, and their actions have shaped the course of Imperial history countless times.

The organization maintains strict protocols and operates under the oversight of the Grand Master of Assassins, who answers directly to the High Lords of Terra. Each successful mission reinforces the Imperium's authority, while failed missions are purged from official records.

Assassin Temples

Temple Vindicare

Masters of the long-range kill, equipped with exotic sniper weapons and stealth technology. Vindicare assassins can wait motionless for weeks to take the perfect shot, their enhanced physiology and training allowing them to operate in any environment.

  • Exitus Rifle - Specialized sniper weapon capable of penetrating any armor
  • Spy Mask - Advanced targeting system with multiple vision modes
  • Stealth Suit - Active camouflage with thermal dampening
  • Exitus Pistol - Backup weapon for close encounters
  • Special Ammunition - Including turbo-penetrator and shield-breaker rounds

Temple Callidus

Shape-shifting infiltrators who can assume any identity. Callidus assassins excel at psychological warfare and can destabilize entire organizations from within.

  • Polymorphine - Shape-changing drug that allows perfect impersonation
  • Neural Shredder - Mind-destroying weapon that leaves no physical trace
  • C'tan Phase Sword - Reality-phasing blade that ignores armor
  • Poison Blades - Various toxic weapons
  • Chameleoline Suit - Additional stealth capabilities

Temple Eversor

Psychotic berserkers enhanced with combat drugs and cybernetics. Kept in cryo-stasis between missions due to their unstable nature.

  • Executioner Pistol - Combined bolt pistol and needle pistol
  • Power Sword - Energy-sheathed blade for close combat
  • Combat Drugs - Cocktail of performance enhancers and stimulants
  • Melta Bombs - For structural damage
  • Self-Destruct Mechanism - Ensures no evidence remains

Temple Culexus

Anti-psyker specialists who terrify and nullify psychic powers. Born as psychic blanks, they are living weapons against warp-based threats.

  • Animus Speculum - Soul-draining helmet that weaponizes their null aura
  • Psyk-out Grenades - Anti-psyker weapons that disrupt warp energy
  • Etherium - Psychic nullification field generator
  • Life Drain - Ability to absorb psychic energy
  • Neural Shredder - Secondary weapon for non-psychic targets

Organization Structure

Grand Master of Assassins

Supreme commander of the Officio Assassinorum, responsible for coordinating all assassination operations and maintaining the organization's secrecy. Reports directly to the High Lords of Terra.

Temple Masters

Leaders of individual temples, each maintaining their temple's unique traditions and training methods. They select and oversee the development of new assassins.

Execution Force

Special operations teams combining assassins from multiple temples for high-priority missions. These rare deployments are reserved for the most dangerous threats.

Notable Assassinations

The Beast War

Multiple temples deployed against Ork leadership during the War of the Beast. A Vindicare assassin successfully eliminated several Ork Warbosses, while Callidus agents sowed confusion among the Ork hierarchy.

Vandire's Fall

Crucial role in ending the Reign of Blood. Multiple assassination attempts culminated in the death of Goge Vandire, ending his tyrannical rule over the Imperium.

The Beheading

A dark chapter in the Officio's history when rogue elements attempted to assassinate the entire High Lords of Terra. Led to major reforms in assassination protocols.

Hive Fleet Leviathan

Culexus assassins deployed against Tyranid Synapse creatures, disrupting the Hive Mind's control over its forces. Contributed to several crucial victories against the Tyranid threat.

Operational Protocols

Deployment Authorization

Strict procedures for assassin deployment, requiring multiple levels of verification and approval. Emergency protocols exist for immediate deployment in critical situations.

Temple Cooperation

Guidelines for multi-temple operations, including chain of command and resource allocation. Execution Forces receive special dispensation for coordinated actions.

Failure Protocols

Detailed procedures for handling failed missions, including evidence elimination and asset recovery. Failed assassins are expected to self-terminate to maintain secrecy.
