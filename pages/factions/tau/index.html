T'au Empire

For the Greater Good - A Young and Ambitious Stellar Empire

Overview

The T'au Empire is one of the youngest major powers in the galaxy, having achieved spaceflight only a few thousand years ago. Despite their relative youth, they have expanded rapidly through their advanced technology, unified society, and the philosophical concept of the Greater Good (T'au'va).

  • Founded: M35-M36
  • Capital: T'au
  • Government: Ethereal-led Caste System
  • Military Doctrine: Combined Arms Warfare

Caste System

Ethereal Caste (Aun)

The ruling caste of the T'au Empire, mysterious leaders who guide their civilization through the philosophy of the Greater Good.

Fire Caste (Shas)

Warriors of the Empire, responsible for military operations and defense. Known for their advanced battlesuits and tactical doctrine.

Earth Caste (Fio)

Engineers, scientists, and builders who create the Empire's advanced technology and infrastructure.

Air Caste (Kor)

Pilots and navigators who command the Empire's fleets and maintain their space presence.

Water Caste (Por)

Diplomats, merchants, and administrators who handle relations with other races and internal governance.

Fifth Sphere

The Greater Good (T'au'va)

The T'au'va is the central philosophy that unites the T'au Empire. It emphasizes:

  • Unity of all races under T'au leadership
  • Progress through technological advancement
  • Sacrifice for the collective good
  • Peaceful integration where possible
  • Martial strength where necessary
