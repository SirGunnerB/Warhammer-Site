Sisters of Battle

The Adepta Sororitas - Brides of the Emperor

Overview

The Adepta Sororitas, also known as the Sisters of Battle, are the military arm of the Ecclesiarchy. They combine unshakeable faith in the Emperor with formidable military prowess, serving as both warriors and defenders of the Imperial Creed.

  • Founded: Age of Apostasy
  • Current Status: Active
  • Organization: Order-based
  • Military Doctrine: Faith and Fire

Major Orders
