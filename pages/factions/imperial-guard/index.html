Imperial Guard

The Hammer of the Emperor - Countless Billions Strong

Overview

The Astra Militarum, commonly known as the Imperial Guard, is the primary fighting force of the Imperium of Man. Comprised of countless billions of soldiers from millions of worlds, they fight through superior numbers, overwhelming firepower, and unshakeable faith in the Emperor.

  • Founded: During the Great Crusade
  • Current Status: Active
  • Organization: Regiment-based
  • Military Doctrine: Combined Arms/Mass Infantry
