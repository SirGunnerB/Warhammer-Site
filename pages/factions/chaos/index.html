Forces of Chaos

The Ruinous Powers - Eternal Enemies of Order

Overview

Chaos represents the greatest threat to the Imperium of Man and all mortal life in the galaxy. It is both a corrupting force and a faction of deadly warriors, daemons, and fallen heroes who have pledged their souls to the Dark Gods.

  • Origin: The Warp/Immaterium
  • Current Status: Active
  • Organization: Chaotic/Warband-based
  • Military Doctrine: Terror/Corruption
