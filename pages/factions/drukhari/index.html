The Drukhari

Masters of Pain, Lords of Commorragh

Overview

The Drukhari, also known as the Dark Eldar, are the dark kin of the Aeldari who reside in the dark city of Commorragh. They sustain themselves on the suffering of others, conducting raids across the galaxy to capture slaves and feed their endless hunger for pain and torment. Unlike their Craftworld cousins, they fully embrace the excesses that led to the Fall, using ancient technology and dark arts to preserve their immortality through the suffering of others.

Society

A complex hierarchy based on power, cunning, and the ability to inflict suffering. Social status is maintained through constant political intrigue and violence.

Technology

Advanced weaponry powered by dark matter and souls, including anti-gravitational devices and weapons that kill through pure pain.

Combat Style

Lightning-fast raids utilizing advanced technology and psychological warfare to terrorize and capture their victims.

Weaknesses

Constant need to replenish their souls through others' suffering, vulnerability to psychic attacks, and internal political strife.

Military Forces

Raiders and Ravagers

The backbone of Drukhari strike forces, these anti-gravitic vessels combine incredible speed with devastating firepower.

  • Raiders: Transport craft equipped with dark lances
  • Ravagers: Heavy gunships with multiple weapon systems
  • Tactics: Hit-and-run attacks, slave capture

Elite Forces

Specialized warriors and assassins who excel in various aspects of combat and terror tactics.

  • Incubi: Elite warrior-mercenaries
  • Mandrakes: Shadow-dwelling assassins
  • Scourges: Winged raiders and scouts

Coven Creations

Twisted biological and mechanical constructs created by the Haemonculi.

  • Grotesques: Mutated combat slaves

  • Cronos: Soul-harvesting constructs
