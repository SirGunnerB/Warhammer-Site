Space Marines

The Emperor's Finest - Superhuman Warriors of the Imperium

Overview

The Adeptus Astartes, commonly known as Space Marines, are the Emperor's finest warriors. These genetically enhanced superhuman soldiers serve as the Imperium's elite fighting force, each worth dozens of normal troops in combat.

  • Founded: Late Age of Strife/Great Crusade
  • Current Status: Active
  • Organization: Chapter System
  • Military Doctrine: Rapid Strike/Shock Assault
