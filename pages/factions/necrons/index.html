Necrons

The Awakened Dynasty - Ancient Masters of the Galaxy

Overview

The Necrons are an ancient race of mechanical warriors who ruled the galaxy millions of years ago. After millennia of slumber, they now emerge from their tomb worlds to reclaim their former empire, armed with technologies far beyond the comprehension of younger races.

  • Origin: Ancient Necrontyr Empire
  • Current Status: Awakening Tomb Worlds
  • Government: Dynastic Hierarchy
  • Military Doctrine: Superior Technology

Major Dynasties

Sautekh Dynasty

Largest and most powerful dynasty, led by Phaeron Imotekh the Stormlord.

Nihilakh Dynasty

Known for their strict codes of honor and territorial defense.

Mephrit Dynasty

Masters of solar weaponry and aggressive expansion.

Szarekhan Dynasty

Dynasty of the Silent King himself, recently returned from exile.

Nephrekh Dynasty

Specialists in dimensional technology and rapid deployment.

Historical Timeline

Age of Flesh

Original Necrontyr civilization, plagued by short lifespans and harsh conditions.

War in Heaven

Conflict with the Old Ones and subsequent alliance with the C'tan.

Bio-transference

Transformation into mechanical bodies and betrayal by the C'tan.

Great Sleep

60 million year hibernation in tomb worlds.

Great Awakening

Current era of reawakening and reclamation.

Notable Constructs

Mobile heavy weapons platforms with extreme destructive capability.
