Overview

Saint Celestine is one of the most revered Living Saints of the Adepta Sororitas, having died and been resurrected countless times through the Emperor's divine power. Her appearances on battlefields across the Imperium have turned the tide of many desperate battles, inspiring the faithful and striking terror into the hearts of heretics.

Known for her golden armor, fiery wings, and the blessed Ardent Blade, Celestine embodies the Emperor's divine will made manifest. Her presence is often seen as a direct sign of the Emperor's favor, and her interventions have saved countless Imperial worlds from destruction.

History

Origins

Before becoming a Living Saint, Celestine was a Sister of Battle in the Order of Our Martyred Lady. Her transformation into a Living Saint occurred after a martyrdom in battle, though the exact circumstances remain shrouded in mystery.

Rise to Sainthood

Following her first resurrection, Celestine began appearing at crucial moments in Imperial history, leading the faithful to victory against impossible odds. Her divine nature became undeniable as she demonstrated the ability to return from death time and time again.

Modern Era

In recent times, Celestine played a crucial role in the defense of Cadia during Abaddon's 13th Black Crusade. Though Cadia ultimately fell, she helped ensure the survival of millions of refugees and later aided Roboute Guilliman during the Terran Crusade.

  • Divine Aura: Her presence bolsters the morale and faith of nearby Imperial forces

Wargear

Legacy

Saint Celestine stands as one of the most powerful symbols of the Emperor's continued presence in the Imperium. Her miraculous appearances and victories have inspired countless faithful and sparked numerous acts of devotion across the galaxy. She represents hope in an age of darkness, proving that the Emperor still watches over humanity.

Her role in recent events, particularly during the Fall of Cadia and the return of Roboute Guilliman, has only enhanced her legendary status. Many see her as living proof that the Emperor continues to protect mankind, even in its darkest hour.
