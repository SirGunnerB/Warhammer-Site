Nathaniel Garro

Knight-Errant Active during Heresy Imperium (Former Death Guard)

Overview

Battle-Captain Nathaniel Garro was a former officer of the Death Guard Legion who became one of the first Knights-Errant. His actions during the early stages of the Horus Heresy proved crucial in warning the Emperor of Horus's betrayal. Known for his unwavering loyalty and dedication to the Imperial Truth, Garro's journey from Death Guard Battle-Captain to founding member of what would become the Grey Knights exemplifies the complex nature of loyalty during the Heresy.

His most famous act was commanding the Eisenstein during its perilous journey to Terra, carrying warning of the Warmaster's betrayal. This action would mark him in Imperial history as one of the greatest heroes of the Horus Heresy, though his subsequent service as a Knight-Errant would prove equally vital to the Emperor's cause.

History

Origins on Terra

Born among the hardy people of Terra's Albian region, Garro was selected for the XIV Legion before the discovery of Mortarion. His Terran heritage would later prove significant in maintaining his loyalty to the Emperor.

Great Crusade Service

Rose through the ranks of the Death Guard, earning his position as Battle-Captain of the 7th Great Company through countless victories. Known for both his martial skill and tactical acumen, he earned the respect of even Mortarion himself.

The Betrayal at Isstvan III

Stationed aboard the Eisenstein during the virus bombing of Isstvan III. Witnessed the betrayal of Horus and made the momentous decision to flee with loyal elements of the Death Guard, including Euphrati Keeler and Iacton Qruze.

Flight of the Eisenstein

Led a harrowing escape through the Warp, facing both daemonic possession and pursuit by the Death Guard vessel Terminus Est. Successfully navigated to Terra through a combination of skill, determination, and sacrifice.

Service to Malcador

After reaching Terra, was recruited by Malcador the Sigillite to become one of the first Knights-Errant. Undertook numerous crucial missions across the galaxy in service to the Emperor.

Formation of the Grey Knights

Played a key role in gathering the founding members of what would become the Grey Knights, though his ultimate fate remains shrouded in mystery.

Notable Battles & Campaigns
