Chaos Lords

Champions of the Dark Gods

Overview

The Lords of Chaos are among the most feared beings in the galaxy, having pledged their souls to the Dark Gods in exchange for power. Many were once noble warriors of the Imperium who fell to corruption, while others were born into the service of Chaos.

Champions of Khorne

Khârn the Betrayer

Former Captain of the World Eaters 8th Company, now the most infamous berserker of Khorne. Known for his bloodthirsty nature and wielding the ancient weapon Gorechild.

Doombreed

One of Khorne's first and most powerful Daemon Princes, elevated to daemonhood long before the birth of the Imperium.

Champions of Tzeentch

Ahriman

Former Chief Librarian of the Thousand Sons, now the most powerful sorcerer of Tzeentch. Seeks to undo the Rubric that turned his brothers to dust.

Kairos Fateweaver

The Oracle of Tzeentch, a two-headed Lord of Change who can see both past and future, but is cursed to never know the present.

Champions of Nurgle

Typhus

Herald of Nurgle and former First Captain of the Death Guard. Now commands the Plague Fleet, spreading disease throughout the galaxy.

Ku'gath Plaguefather

A Great Unclean One who seeks to create the perfect disease, testing his concoctions on mortal worlds.

Champions of Slaanesh

Lucius the Eternal

Former Captain of the Emperor's Children, cursed to resurrect through those who take pride in killing him.

N'Kari

A Keeper of Secrets who has terrorized the galaxy since the Great Crusade, known for hunting the bloodlines of those who have wronged it.

Champions of Chaos Undivided

Abaddon the Despoiler

Warmaster of Chaos and former First Captain of the Sons of Horus. Leader of the Black Legion and architect of the Black Crusades.

Be'lakor

The First Daemon Prince, cursed by the Gods to never achieve the power he craves, yet still one of the most powerful beings in existence.

Notable Campaigns

  • The 13th Black Crusade - Led by Abaddon, resulting in the fall of Cadia
  • The Crimson Path - Khârn's bloody rampage through Imperial space
  • The Plague Wars - Typhus's campaign alongside Mortarion in Ultramar
  • The Rubric of Ahriman - The catastrophic spell that changed the Thousand Sons forever
