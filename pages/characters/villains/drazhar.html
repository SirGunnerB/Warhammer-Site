Drazhar

Master of Blades Active Incubi

Overview

Drazhar, the Master of Blades, is the most lethal warrior among the Dark Eldar Incubi. Some whisper he might be the Phoenix Lord Arhra, first of the Striking Scorpions, fallen to darkness. His skill with a blade is unmatched in Commorragh, and his very presence strikes terror into the hearts of his enemies.

History

Mysterious Origins

Appeared suddenly in Commorragh, challenging and slaying the ruling Hierarch of the Incubi in single combat.

Rise to Power

Refused the position of Hierarch, instead taking the title "Master of Blades" and becoming the deadly example all Incubi aspire to match.

Legend of Arhra

Many believe him to be Arhra, the fallen Phoenix Lord of the Striking Scorpions, though he has never confirmed or denied these claims.

Current Era

Continues to serve as the ultimate warrior of the Incubi, taking to the field only in the most worthy battles.
