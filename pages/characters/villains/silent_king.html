Szarekh, The Silent King

Last King of the Necrontyr Active Necron Empire

Overview

Szarekh, known as the Silent King, was the last ruler of the Necrontyr and remains the supreme overlord of the Necron race. His decisions shaped the destiny of his entire species, leading them through biotransference and eventually into their current awakening. After millennia of self-imposed exile, he has returned to unite his people against the growing threats to the galaxy.

History

Necrontyr Era

Led the Necrontyr empire during its war against the Old Ones, ultimately making the fateful decision to accept the C'tan's offer of biotransference.

War in Heaven

Commanded Necron forces during the War in Heaven, eventually leading the rebellion against the C'tan and ordering their imprisonment in tesseract labyrinths.

The Great Sleep

Ordered the Great Sleep but chose exile instead, wandering the void between galaxies while contemplating his species' fate.

Return

Returned to the galaxy after encountering the Tyranid threat, working to unite the Necron dynasties against this existential danger.
