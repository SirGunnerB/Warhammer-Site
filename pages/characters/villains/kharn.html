Khârn the Betrayer

Champion of Khorne Active World Eaters/Khorne

Overview

Khârn the Betrayer is the most infamous berserker of Khorne and former Captain of the World Eaters' 8th Assault Company. His bloodthirsty actions have earned him the title "The Betrayer" and made him one of the most feared warriors in the galaxy.

History

Great Crusade

Served as a respected officer in the World Eaters Legion, known for both martial skill and tactical acumen.

Horus Heresy

Followed Angron into rebellion, becoming increasingly violent after receiving the Butcher's Nails.

Skalathrax

Earned the title "The Betrayer" after turning on his own allies during the Battle of Skalathrax.

Current Era

Continues to spread bloodshed across the galaxy as Khorne's favored champion.
