Illuminor Szeras

The Illuminor Active Independent Necron

Overview

Illuminor Szeras is one of the most brilliant and ruthless minds among the Necrons. His endless quest for knowledge and understanding of life itself has led him to perform countless experiments on living subjects, all in pursuit of the secrets of evolution and consciousness.

History

Pre-Biotransference

One of the leading scientists among the Necrontyr, instrumental in the development of biotransference technology alongside the C'tan.

The Great Sleep

Maintained consciousness during portions of the Great Sleep, continuing his research and modifications on himself and others.

Awakening

Among the first to awaken, immediately resuming his experiments and research into the nature of life and consciousness.

Current Era

Conducts extensive research across the galaxy, offering his services to various Necron dynasties in exchange for research subjects and resources.
