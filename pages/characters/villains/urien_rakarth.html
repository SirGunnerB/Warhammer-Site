Urien Rakarth

Master Haemonculus Active Prophets of Flesh

Overview

Urien Rakarth, known as the Master Haemonculus and the Sculptor of Torments, is the most brilliant and twisted flesh-sculptor in Commorragh. His mastery of pain and transformation has made him both feared and sought after throughout the Dark City.

History

Early Years

One of the original Haemonculi, present during the founding of the Dark City. Pioneered many of the flesh-crafting techniques still used today.

Rise to Power

Established the Prophets of Flesh coven, developing unprecedented mastery over flesh-crafting and regeneration techniques.

Countless Deaths

Has died and been reborn countless times, each resurrection bringing new insights into the nature of pain and transformation.

Current Era

Continues to push the boundaries of flesh-crafting, creating ever more horrific and "perfect" creations.
