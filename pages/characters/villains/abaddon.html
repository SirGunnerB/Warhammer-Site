Abaddon the Despoiler

Warmaster of Chaos Active Black Legion

Overview

Ezekyle Abaddon, now known as Abaddon the Despoiler, is the Warmaster of Chaos and leader of the Black Legion. Once First Captain of the Luna Wolves Legion and right hand of Horus himself, he has become the greatest champion of Chaos Undivided and architect of the Long War against the Imperium.

History

Great Crusade

First Captain of the Luna Wolves, leader of the elite Justaerin Terminator Squad, and most favored son of Horus. Participated in countless victories during the Great Crusade.

Horus Heresy

Fought at the forefront of the Heresy, leading many crucial battles. Present at the Siege of Terra and witnessed Horus's death at the Emperor's hands.

Rise to Power

Reformed the Sons of Horus into the Black Legion, refusing to simply succeed Horus but instead forging his own path as Warmaster of Chaos.

Black Crusades

Led thirteen Black Crusades against the Imperium, culminating in the destruction of Cadia and the creation of the Great Rift in the 13th Black Crusade.

Leadership & Command

The Black Legion

Transformed the broken Sons of Horus into the mighty Black Legion, united under his banner and dedicated to the Long War against the Imperium.

Command Style

Rules through a combination of fear, respect, and strategic brilliance. Unlike Horus, he refuses godhood and maintains his independence from the Chaos Gods while using their power.
