Typhus

Herald of Nurgle Active Death Guard/Nurgle

Overview

Formerly known as Calas Typhon, Typhus is the Herald of Nurgle and host of the Destroyer Hive. His betrayal of the Death Guard Legion led to their corruption by Nurgle, and he continues to spread plague and decay across the galaxy.

History

Early Life

Born on Barbarus, descended from the planet's witch-kings, concealed his psychic abilities.

Great Crusade

Rose to become First Captain of the Death Guard, secretly practiced forbidden sorcery.

The Betrayal

Trapped the Death Guard fleet in the Warp, leading to their corruption by Nurgle.

Current Era

Leads plague fleets across the galaxy, spreading Nurgle's gifts.
