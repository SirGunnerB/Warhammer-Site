Erebus

First Chaplain Active Word Bearers/Chaos

Overview

Erebus was the First Chaplain of the Word Bearers Legion and one of the primary architects of the Horus Heresy. His actions and manipulations led to the corruption of Lorgar and ultimately Horus, setting in motion the greatest civil war in human history.

History

Early Life

Originally from Colchis, murdered and stole the identity of a man named Erebus to join the Word Bearers.

Great Crusade

Rose to become First Chaplain, secretly worshipped the Chaos Gods while spreading the Imperial Truth.

Corruption of Lorgar

Instrumental in turning Lorgar to Chaos after the destruction of Monarchia.

Horus Heresy

Manipulated events leading to Horus's fall, including the events on Davin.
