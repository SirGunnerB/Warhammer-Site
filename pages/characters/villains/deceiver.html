The Deceiver

C'tan Shard Active Self

Overview

Mephet'ran the Deceiver is one of the most infamous C'tan, known for his mastery of manipulation and deception. Though now shattered into shards like his fellow C'tan, each fragment retains his legendary cunning and ability to twist truth and reality to serve his own ends.

History

The War in Heaven

Played a crucial role in convincing the Necrontyr to undergo biotransference, transforming them into the soulless Necrons while feeding on their essence.

The Great Sleep

Unlike other C'tan, many believe the Deceiver remained partially active during the Great Sleep, influencing various races and civilizations.

The Shattering

Shattered into shards by the revolting Necrons, though each shard maintains significant power and the core aspects of his nature.

Current Era

His shards continue to manipulate events across the galaxy, each fragment pursuing its own inscrutable agenda.
