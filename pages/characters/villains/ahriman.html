Ahzek Ahriman

Chief Librarian Active Thousand Sons/Tzeentch

Overview

Ahzek Ahriman is the former Chief Librarian of the Thousand Sons Legion and one of the most powerful sorcerers in the galaxy. His pursuit of knowledge and attempt to save his Legion led to the disastrous Rubric of Ahriman, which turned most of his brothers into dust-filled automata.

History

Early Life

Born on Prospero, demonstrated exceptional psychic potential from an early age.

Great Crusade

Served as Chief Librarian, helped develop the Thousand Sons' psychic disciplines.

Burning of Prospero

Fought to defend his homeworld from the Space Wolves, witnessed its destruction.

The Rubric

Cast the infamous Rubric of Ahriman, attempting to stop the flesh-change but damning his Legion.

Exile

Banished by Magnus, now seeks to perfect the Rubric and gather forbidden knowledge.
