Lucius the Eternal

Champion of Slaanesh Eternal Emperor's Children/Slaanesh

Overview

Lucius the Eternal is a Champion of Slaanesh and former Captain of the Emperor's Children Legion. Blessed (or cursed) by Slaanesh with immortality, he returns to life by possessing anyone who takes pride in killing him, their body transforming into his own.

History

Early Life

Rose through the ranks of the Emperor's Children due to his exceptional swordsmanship.

Great Crusade

Known as the best swordsman in the Legion, developed infamous dueling reputation.

Horus Heresy

Followed Fulgrim into corruption, embracing excess and perfection in combat.

Slaanesh's Gift

Granted immortality by Slaanesh, beginning his eternal cycle of death and rebirth.
