Asdrubael Vect

Supreme Overlord of Commorragh Active Kabal of the Black Heart

Overview

Asdrubael Vect is the Supreme Overlord of Commorragh and leader of the Kabal of the Black Heart. Through millennia of plotting and manipulation, he has risen from alleged slavery to become the most powerful being in the Dark City, repeatedly demonstrating his mastery of political intrigue and survival.

History

Ancient Times

Claims to have witnessed the Fall of the Eldar, though the truth of this is debated. Rose from supposed slavery to power through cunning and manipulation.

Rise to Power

Orchestrated the destruction of the noble houses of Commorragh, establishing the Kabal system and his own supremacy.

Consolidation

Maintained power through countless assassination attempts and coup plots, always emerging stronger through elaborate schemes.

Recent Events

Staged his own death and resurrection, further cementing his power and mystique in Commorragh.

Rule of Commorragh

Leadership Style

Rules through a complex web of manipulation, fear, and carefully orchestrated power plays. Maintains control by keeping all potential rivals in constant conflict with each other.

Political System

Created and maintains the Kabal system, ensuring no single power can grow strong enough to challenge his rule while maintaining the appearance of opportunity for advancement.
