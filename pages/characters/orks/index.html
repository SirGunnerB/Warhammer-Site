Ork Warlords

The Biggest and Baddest

Overview

Ork Warlords are the largest, strongest, and most cunning of their species. These mighty warriors rise to power through sheer force and maintain their position by continuing to prove their might in battle. Each has led massive WAAAGH!s that have terrorized the galaxy.

Legendary Warbosses

Ghazghkull Mag Uruk Thraka

The Prophet of Gork and Mork, greatest Ork Warlord since the Beast. Led multiple devastating wars for Armageddon and continues to unite more Orks under his banner.

  • Self-proclaimed prophet of the Ork gods
  • Mastermind of the Second and Third Wars for Armageddon
  • Survived multiple attempts on his life
  • Constantly growing in size and power

The Beast

Ancient Warlord who nearly conquered Terra itself during the War of the Beast. His technological and organizational abilities far exceeded normal Ork capabilities.

Notable Current Warbosses

Nazdreg Ug Urdgrub

Unusually subtle for an Ork, known for his cunning strategies and trading with other races. Leader of the Bad Moons clan.

Boss Zagstruk

Leader of the "Vulcha Squad," famous for his aerial assaults using modified Rokkit Packs.

Kaptin Badrukk

Self-proclaimed "Flash Git King," known for his expensive tastes and advanced weaponry.

Specialist Warbosses

Mad Dok Grotsnik

Deranged Pain Boy whose "improvements" are feared even by other Orks, but whose results cannot be denied.

Big Mek Buzzgob

Renowned Mekboy known for creating massive war machines and fortifications.
