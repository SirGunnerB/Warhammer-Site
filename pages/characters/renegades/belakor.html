Be'lakor

The First Prince Active Chaos Undivided

Overview

Be'lakor, the Dark Master, holds the distinction of being the first mortal to be elevated to daemonhood. Once a favored champion of Chaos Undivided, his pride and ambition led to his downfall, cursed by the Dark Gods to forever crown others but never rule himself.

History

Ancient Times

Rose to power in a long-forgotten age, becoming the first mortal to achieve daemonhood through the favor of all four Chaos Gods.

The Curse

His ambition and pride led to his downfall, cursed by the Chaos Gods to crown their champions but never to rule himself.

Through History

Manipulated events from the shadows, seeking to break his curse while fulfilling his role in crowning new champions.

Modern Era

Continues to scheme and plot, leading armies of darkness while searching for ways to break free of his constraints.

Role in Chaos

The Dark Coronator

Forced to crown champions of Chaos, Be'lakor plays a crucial role in the ascension of new Daemon Princes while being denied the power to rule himself.

Master of Shadows

Commands vast armies of shadow daemons and manipulates events from behind the scenes, working through proxies and possessed pawns.
