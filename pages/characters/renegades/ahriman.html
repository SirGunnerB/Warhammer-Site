Ahriman

Arch-Sorcerer of Tzeentch Active Independent/Thousand Sons

Overview

Ahzek Ahriman, once Chief Librarian of the Thousand Sons Legion, is now one of the most powerful sorcerers in the galaxy. His pursuit of knowledge and attempt to save his Legion through the Rubric has made him both legendary and infamous throughout the millennia.

History

Great Crusade

Served as Chief Librarian of the Thousand Sons, helping to master and control the Legion's psychic powers while gathering knowledge across the galaxy.

The Rubric

Cast the infamous Rubric of Ahriman, attempting to stop the flesh-change but instead turning most of his brothers into dust-filled automata.

Exile

Banished by Magnus the Red, began a quest across time and space to gather knowledge and potentially reverse the Rubric's effects.

Current Era

Continues his pursuits of arcane knowledge and power, leading his own warband while maintaining complex schemes across the galaxy.
