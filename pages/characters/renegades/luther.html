Luther

The Arch-Betrayer Active Fallen Angels

Overview

Luther was once the closest friend and mentor to Lion El'Jonson, and a founding member of the Order on Caliban. His eventual betrayal of the Lion and the Dark Angels Legion marked one of the most tragic moments in the chapter's history, leading to the destruction of Caliban and the creation of the Fallen Angels.

History

Early Life

A noble knight of The Order on Caliban, Luther discovered and raised the young Lion El'Jonson. Together they reformed The Order and purged Caliban of its great beasts.

Great Crusade

Initially passed over for Space Marine enhancement due to age, Luther was later partially enhanced and served as second-in-command of the Dark Angels Legion.

The Betrayal

Sent back to Caliban in perceived disgrace, Luther's resentment grew. He eventually turned against the Lion, embracing forbidden knowledge and leading half the Legion in rebellion.

Modern Era

Imprisoned within the Rock for millennia, Luther has escaped multiple times. His prophecies and actions continue to influence both the Dark Angels and the Fallen.
