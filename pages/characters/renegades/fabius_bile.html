Fabius Bile

The Clonelord Active Independent/Chaos

Overview

Fabius Bile, once Chief Apothecary of the Emperor's Children Legion, is now known as the Clonelord, the Primogenitor, and the Spider of Chaos. His obsession with genetic manipulation and "improving" the human form has made him both reviled and sought after throughout the galaxy.

History

Great Crusade

Served as Chief Apothecary of the Emperor's Children, working to cure the Blight that afflicted his Legion. His early experiments laid the groundwork for his later obsessions.

Horus Heresy

Played a crucial role in the corruption of the Emperor's Children, perfecting combat stimulants and conducting increasingly extreme experiments on both Marines and civilians.

Post-Heresy

Established multiple hidden laboratories across the galaxy, creating the New Men and attempting to clone Primarchs. Successfully created the "Gland-hounds" and other enhanced warriors.

Current Era

Continues his work through a network of clones, pursuing genetic perfection while dealing with various factions of both Chaos and the Imperium.

Notable Experiments

The New Men

Enhanced humans designed to be the next step in evolution, though most attempts resulted in failure or mutation.

Primarch Cloning

Multiple attempts to clone Primarchs, including Horus and Fulgrim, with varying degrees of success and catastrophic failures.

Gland-hounds

Enhanced human hunters with heightened senses and combat abilities, used to track down specific genetic sequences.

The Consortium

A network of clones maintaining his work across the galaxy, each believing themselves to be the original Fabius Bile.
