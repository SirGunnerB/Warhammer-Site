Cypher

Lord of the Fallen Active Unknown

Overview

Cypher is one of the most enigmatic figures in the Imperium, a mysterious warrior who may have originated from the Dark Angels Legion. He carries a sword he never draws and appears throughout history at pivotal moments, his true motives unknown.
