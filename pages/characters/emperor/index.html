The Emperor of Mankind

Master of Mankind, Lord of Terra, Omnissiah

Overview

The Emperor of Mankind is the immortal ruling monarch of the Imperium of Man, and is described as the greatest and most powerful human psyker ever born. For most of humanity's history, He has guided mankind from the shadows, but after the Age of Strife, He revealed Himself to unite Terra and forge the Imperium of Man.

History

Ancient History

Born in ancient times in central Anatolia on Earth, the being who would become the Emperor was the collective reincarnation of thousands of ancient human shamans. Through millennia, He guided humanity from the shadows, taking different identities throughout human history.

The Great Crusade

In M30, He emerged to unite the warring nations of Terra, created the Space Marines and the Primarchs, and launched the Great Crusade to reunite humanity across the galaxy. This marked the beginning of the Imperium of Man.

The Horus Heresy

The Emperor's most favored son, Horus, betrayed him, leading to the devastating Horus Heresy. The conflict culminated in a final battle aboard Horus's flagship, where the Emperor was mortally wounded but succeeded in defeating Horus.

Powers and Abilities

  • Immortality and Perpetual nature
  • Immense psychic powers
  • Superhuman physical abilities
  • Vast intellectual and tactical genius
  • Ability to create and manipulate life (demonstrated in the creation of the Primarchs)

Current State

After His devastating injuries in the battle with Horus, the Emperor was placed upon the Golden Throne, where He remains to this day, sustaining the Astronomican and holding back the forces of Chaos from breaching into realspace through the Imperial Webway gate beneath the Imperial Palace.

Imperial Cult

Though the Emperor originally opposed religious worship, after His internment on the Golden Throne, the Imperial Cult arose, declaring Him a god. This religion now forms the state church of the Imperium, with the Emperor worshipped as the God-Emperor of Mankind.

Notable Quotes
