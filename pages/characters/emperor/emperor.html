The Emperor of Mankind

Master of Mankind Enthroned Imperium of Man

Overview

The Emperor of Mankind is the immortal ruling monarch of the Imperium of Man, and is described as the Father, Guardian, and God of humanity. He has sat immobile, his body slowly crumbling, within the Golden Throne of Terra for over 10,000 years. Although physically all but dead, his will extends through the Immaterium and spans the entire Imperium.

Known by many names throughout history - the Master of Mankind, the Omnissiah, the Allfather, He Who Sits on Terra - the Emperor represents humanity's greatest hope for survival in a galaxy filled with countless horrors.

Origins and Early History

The First Men

Born around 8,000 BCE in ancient Anatolia, the being who would become the Emperor was the collective reincarnation of thousands of ancient human shamans. These psykers, foreseeing humanity's doom, performed a ritual of mass suicide to create a being of unprecedented psychic power.

Ancient Times

  • Guided human development through millennia
  • Took various identities throughout history
  • Influenced major historical events
  • Fought against Chaos corruption

The Age of Strife

During the Age of Strife, as human civilization collapsed, the Emperor remained hidden, gathering power and knowledge. He foresaw that humanity would need his direct intervention to survive the coming storms.

Rise to Power

Unification Wars (M30)

The Emperor emerged publicly, creating the Thunder Warriors and beginning the reunification of Terra. He conquered the techno-barbarians and brought order to humanity's cradle.

  • Creation of the Thunder Warriors
  • Conquest of Terra's warring nations
  • Alliance with Mars and the Mechanicum
  • Development of Space Marine technology

The Primarch Project

Created twenty superhuman sons using his own genetic material. The Primarchs were scattered across the galaxy by the Chaos Gods, leading to a galaxy-wide quest to recover them.

  • Genetic mastery demonstration
  • Creation of twenty unique beings
  • Loss and recovery missions
  • Foundation of Space Marine Legions

Great Crusade

Led humanity's greatest expansion, reuniting lost human worlds and establishing the Imperium of Man. Found his scattered sons and built an empire spanning the galaxy.

  • Recovery of the Primarchs
  • Establishment of the Imperium
  • Conquest of countless worlds
  • Creation of Imperial institutions

The Webway Project

Secretly worked to create human access to the Eldar Webway, aiming to free humanity from reliance on Warp travel. This project's interruption proved catastrophic.

Horus Heresy

Faced the greatest betrayal as Horus led half the Primarchs and their Legions in rebellion. The final battle left the Emperor mortally wounded and forever changed the Imperium.

  • Battle against Chaos corruption
  • Loss of beloved sons
  • Final confrontation with Horus
  • Internment on Golden Throne

The Golden Throne

Current State

For over 10,000 years, the Emperor has been sustained by the Golden Throne, a complex life support system and psychic amplifier. His consciousness endures while his physical form slowly decays.

Daily Sacrifice

  • Requires thousands of psykers daily
  • Powers the Astronomican

Imperial Faith

Despite his opposition to religion during the Great Crusade, the Emperor is now worshipped as a god by the Imperial Cult, his words and actions interpreted as divine prophecy.
