Aeldari Heroes

Champions of the Ancient Race

Overview

The Aeldari, an ancient race whose empire once spanned the galaxy, continue to produce legendary heroes who fight to prevent both their species' extinction and the triumph of Chaos. These individuals exemplify the highest achievements of Aeldari civilization.

Craftworld Heroes

Eldrad Ulthran

Former Chief Farseer of Craftworld Ulthw√©, one of the most powerful psykers in the galaxy. His prophecies have saved countless lives and shaped major events in galactic history.

Prince Yriel

Autarch of Iyanden and High Admiral of the Eldritch Raiders. Wielder of the Spear of Twilight, he saved his craftworld from Hive Fleet Kraken.

Jain Zar

The Storm of Silence, first of the Howling Banshees, and one of the Phoenix Lords. She continues to train new generations of Aspect Warriors.

The Phoenix Lords

Asurmen

The Hand of Asuryan, first of the Phoenix Lords and founder of the Aspect Warriors. He created the first Aspect Shrine and trained the other Phoenix Lords.

Karandras

The Shadow Hunter, Phoenix Lord of the Striking Scorpions. Known for his patience and lethal precision in combat.

Maugan Ra

The Harvester of Souls, Phoenix Lord of the Dark Reapers. Single-handedly saved his craftworld from the Tyranids.

Harlequin Characters

Shadowseer Sylandri Veilwalker

A mysterious Shadowseer who has manipulated events throughout the galaxy, including the resurrection of Roboute Guilliman.

The Masked One

A legendary Solitaire who has performed the Dance of the Youngest God countless times, each performance bringing terror to enemies of the Aeldari.

Ynnari Leaders

Yvraine

The Emissary of Ynnead, herald of the Aeldari God of Death. Her actions have begun a new chapter in Aeldari history.

The Visarch

Sword of Ynnead and protector of Yvraine, formerly an Aspect Warrior of the Biel-Tan Craftworld.

Notable Achievements

  • Eldrad's prophecies preventing numerous catastrophes
  • The defense of Iyanden against Hive Fleet Kraken
  • The resurrection of Roboute Guilliman
  • The formation of the Ynnari and the awakening of Ynnead
