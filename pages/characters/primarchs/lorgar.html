Overview

Lorgar Aurelian, known as the Urizen and Bearer of the Word, was the Primarch of the Word Bearers Legion and the first of the Emperor's sons to secretly turn to Chaos. A deeply spiritual being, Lorgar's quest for divine truth led him from worshipping the Emperor as a god to embracing the Ruinous Powers of Chaos, ultimately becoming the architect of the Horus Heresy.

His actions in spreading both the Imperial Cult and later the worship of Chaos would have far-reaching consequences for the galaxy. The religious empire he built would ironically become the foundation of the Imperial Creed, while his secret conversion to Chaos would spark the greatest civil war in human history.

Early Life

Life on Colchis

Landing on the feudal world of Colchis, Lorgar was found and raised by Kor Phaeron, a high ranking priest of the Covenant. Under his mentor's guidance, Lorgar quickly rose through the ranks of the predominant religion, using his charisma and oratory skills to unite the planet in a holy war against the 'unwbelievers'. His natural ability to inspire faith and devotion made him a powerful religious figure.

Meeting the Emperor

When the Emperor arrived on Colchis, Lorgar immediately recognized him as the divine being he had seen in his visions. He swiftly pledged his allegiance and accepted his role as a Primarch, though he would continue to preach the Emperor's divinity despite the Imperial Truth's secular nature. This devotion would eventually lead to his censure and fall from grace.

Great Crusade

Leading the Word Bearers

Taking command of the XVII Legion, renamed the Word Bearers, Lorgar transformed them into zealous missionaries of the Imperial Truth, though secretly preaching the Emperor's divinity. His conquests were marked by their thoroughness, as he would not merely conquer worlds but convert their entire populations to the worship of the Emperor.

The Rebuke

The Emperor's censure of Lorgar and the Word Bearers at Monarchia, where an entire city built in the Emperor's honor was destroyed as a lesson against religious worship, shattered Lorgar's faith. This event would drive him to seek other gods, eventually leading him to the powers of Chaos during the Pilgrimage.

The Horus Heresy

Architect of Heresy

Lorgar was the first Primarch to turn to Chaos, spending decades secretly preparing for the Heresy. He orchestrated the corruption of Horus and manipulated events to ensure the spread of Chaos throughout the Legions. His Legion's actions, particularly at Calth and during the Shadow Crusade, were crucial in dividing the Imperium's forces.

The Shadow Crusade

Alongside Angron and the World Eaters, Lorgar led a devastating campaign through Ultramar, weakening Guilliman's realm while simultaneously engineering Angron's ascension to daemonhood. This campaign showcased both his tactical abilities and his deep understanding of Chaos's corrupting power.

Legacy

Lorgar's influence on the Imperium is deeply ironic - the Imperial Cult he created, which led to his censure, would become the state religion of the Imperium he sought to destroy. His writings and philosophies continue to inspire both loyal Imperial citizens and Chaos cultists alike.

As a Daemon Prince, he remains secluded in his temple on Sicarus, the daemon world capital of his Legion. His long period of meditation has led to various interpretations of his intentions, while his Legion continues to spread chaos and corruption throughout the galaxy.
