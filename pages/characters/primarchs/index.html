The Primarchs

The Emperor's Greatest Creations

Overview

The Primarchs were twenty genetically-engineered superhumans created by the Emperor of Mankind to lead His Great Crusade and reunite the scattered human worlds into the Imperium of Man. Each Primarch was created with unique abilities and characteristics, and would later serve as the genetic template for their respective Space Marine Legions.

Current Status

Of the original twenty Primarchs, many were lost during the Horus Heresy or in its aftermath. Currently, only Roboute Guilliman has returned to lead the Imperium as its Lord Commander, while most others are either confirmed dead, missing, or serving the Ruinous Powers of Chaos.

Legacy

The genetic legacy of the Primarchs lives on in their respective Space Marine Chapters. Each Chapter maintains the traditions, combat doctrines, and characteristics of their Primarch, even ten thousand years after the Horus Heresy.
