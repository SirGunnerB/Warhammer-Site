Jaghatai Khan

Primarch Missing Imperium

Overview

Jaghatai Khan, known as the Great Khan and the Warhawk, was the Primarch of the White Scars Legion. Found on the feudal world of Chogoris (also known as Mundus Planus), he united the warring tribes under his banner and created an empire based on swift strikes and mobile warfare. His mastery of lightning warfare and hit-and-run tactics would become legendary throughout the Great Crusade.

Unlike many of his brother Primarchs, the Khan maintained a degree of independence and cultural identity for his Legion, preserving the traditions of Chogoris while serving the Emperor's vision. His disappearance into the Webway while pursuing Dark Eldar raiders remains one of the great mysteries of the Imperium.

History

Early Life on Chogoris

Landing in the steppes of Chogoris, the young Primarch was found by a tribe of nomadic warriors. Named after a mythical warlord, Jaghatai quickly mastered the arts of war and horsemanship, becoming a legendary figure among his people. His tactical genius and leadership abilities allowed him to unite the nomadic tribes against their oppressors.

Unification of Chogoris

Leading his coalition of tribes, Jaghatai waged war against the settled empire of Palatine, defeating their armies through superior mobility and tactical innovation. His conquest united the entire planet under his rule, bringing peace and prosperity to a formerly divided world.

Discovery by the Emperor

When the Emperor arrived on Chogoris, He found a world already united under the Khan's leadership. Recognizing his father immediately, Jaghatai pledged his service to the Great Crusade while maintaining his people's cultural identity and traditions.

Great Crusade

During the Great Crusade, the Khan transformed the V Legion into the White Scars, incorporating the warrior traditions and mobile warfare tactics of Chogoris. His Legion became renowned for their lightning-fast attacks and mastery of hit-and-run warfare, earning both respect and suspicion from their fellow Legions.

Horus Heresy

When the Heresy erupted, the Khan was initially isolated from Terra. After careful consideration and a confrontation with Mortarion, he chose loyalty to the Emperor. His Legion's speed and mobility proved crucial in many battles, including the defense of Terra during the final siege.

Disappearance

After the Heresy, while pursuing Dark Eldar raiders who had been plaguing Imperial worlds, Jaghatai Khan disappeared into the Webway. Despite numerous search efforts by the White Scars, his ultimate fate remains unknown.
