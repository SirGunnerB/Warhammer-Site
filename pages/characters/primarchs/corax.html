Corvus Corax

Primarch Active in the Warp Imperium

Overview

Corvus Corax, known as the Raven Lord and Deliverer, was the Primarch of the Raven Guard Legion. Landing on the prison moon of Lycaeus orbiting Kiavahr, he led a successful slave rebellion against their tech-guild oppressors. His mastery of stealth, guerrilla warfare, and hit-and-run tactics made him and his Legion unique among the Emperor's forces.

History

Life on Lycaeus

Raised among slave-miners, Corax learned the art of stealth and sabotage. He led a successful rebellion against the tech-guilds, liberating the moon's population with minimal casualties.

Great Crusade

Applied guerrilla warfare tactics to planetary conquest, emphasizing precision strikes and minimal civilian casualties. His Legion became masters of stealth and rapid assault.

Horus Heresy

Survived the Dropsite Massacre on Isstvan V, though his Legion was nearly destroyed. Later attempted to rebuild using accelerated gene-seed processes, with tragic results.

Post-Heresy

Consumed by guilt over his failed attempts to rebuild his Legion, Corax entered the Eye of Terror to hunt traitors. He continues his vendetta, now appearing as a being of pure shadow.
