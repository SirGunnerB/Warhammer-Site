T'au Commanders

Leaders of the Greater Good

Overview

The T'au Empire is led by skilled commanders who combine tactical brilliance with advanced battlesuit technology. These leaders have expanded the Empire through both diplomatic means and military conquest, all in service of the Greater Good.

Ethereal Leadership

Aun'Va

The Master of the Undying Spirit, former leader of the Ethereal Supreme Council. Though his physical form was destroyed, his presence continues through advanced holographic technology.

Aun'Shi

A legendary Ethereal known for his martial prowess, having mastered the art of the Honor Blade. His actions have inspired countless Fire Warriors.

Notable Commanders

Commander Shadowsun

O'Shaserra, the supreme commander of the T'au military forces. Master of the Kauyon (Patient Hunter) tactical doctrine and wielder of prototype battlesuit technology.

  • Supreme Commander of the Fire Caste
  • Pioneer of stealth tactics
  • Leader of the Third Sphere Expansion
  • Master of the XV22 Battlesuit

Commander Farsight

O'Shovah, the legendary rebel commander who established the Farsight Enclaves. Wields the Dawn Blade and questions the Ethereals' control.

  • Master of the Mont'ka (Killing Blow) doctrine
  • Leader of the Farsight Enclaves
  • Legendary battlesuit pilot
  • Bearer of the mysterious Dawn Blade

Other Notable Heroes

Commander Puretide

The legendary teacher of many great commanders, including Shadowsun and Farsight. His tactical teachings live on through advanced AI engrams.

Commander Brightsword

O'Kais, one of Puretide's three most famous pupils, known for his aggressive combat style and mastery of the Mont'ka.

Commander Longstrike

The T'au Empire's most gifted Hammerhead tank ace, whose targeting abilities are said to rival battlesuit systems.

Notable Campaigns

  • The Damocles Gulf Crusade - Shadowsun's defense against Imperial forces
  • The Farsight Expedition - Leading to the establishment of the Farsight Enclaves
  • The Third Sphere Expansion - Led by Commander Shadowsun
  • The Battle of Mu'gulath Bay - A significant victory for the Empire

Advanced Technology

  • XV22 Stealth Battlesuit - Shadowsun's personal armor
  • XV8 Crisis Battlesuit - Standard commander battlesuit
  • XV85 Enforcer Battlesuit - Advanced command variant
  • XV86 Coldstar Battlesuit - High-mobility variant
