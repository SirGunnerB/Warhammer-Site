Notable Space Marines

Heroes and Legends of the Adeptus Astartes

Overview

Throughout the long history of the Imperium, many Space Marines have risen to legendary status through their heroic deeds, tactical brilliance, and unwavering loyalty to the Emperor. These warriors have shaped the destiny of their Chapters and the Imperium itself.

Ultramarines

Marneus Calgar

Chapter Master of the Ultramarines, Lord of Macragge, and one of the greatest tactical minds in the Imperium. He has led the Ultramarines through countless victories and survived seemingly fatal wounds multiple times.

Cato Sicarius

Captain of the 2nd Company, Grand Duke of Talassar, and one of the most skilled swordsmen in the Chapter. Known for his supreme confidence and exceptional combat abilities.

Blood Angels

Dante

Chapter Master of the Blood Angels and the oldest serving Space Marine in the Imperium. His leadership spans over 1,100 years, making him one of the most experienced commanders in the galaxy.

Mephiston

Chief Librarian of the Blood Angels, known as the Lord of Death. He is one of the most powerful psykers in the Imperium and the only Blood Angel known to have overcome the Black Rage.

Space Wolves

Logan Grimnar

Great Wolf of the Space Wolves, he has led his Chapter for over 700 years. Known for his fierce independence and unwavering protection of humanity.

Bjorn the Fell-Handed

The oldest living Space Marine, now interred within a Dreadnought. He fought alongside Leman Russ during the Great Crusade and Horus Heresy.

Dark Angels

Azrael

Supreme Grand Master of the Dark Angels, keeper of the Chapter's darkest secrets, and one of the most respected Chapter Masters in the Imperium.

Ezekiel

Grand Master of Librarians, keeper of the Book of Salvation, and one of the most powerful psykers among the Dark Angels.

Imperial Fists

Vladimir Pugh

Former Chapter Master of the Imperial Fists, known for his steadfast leadership and tactical expertise in siege warfare.

Lysander

Captain of the 1st Company, who returned to his Chapter after being lost in the Warp for over a thousand years.
