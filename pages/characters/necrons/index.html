Necron Overlords

Lords of the Ancient Dynasties

Overview

The Necron Overlords are the rulers of the ancient Necron dynasties, beings who traded their mortality for eternal life in bodies of living metal. They maintain their ancient personalities and ambitions, though many were driven mad by the biotransference process.

The Silent King

Szarekh

The last Silent King of the Necrontyr and architect of their transformation into the Necrons. After millennia of self-imposed exile, he has returned to unite his people against the Tyranid threat.

  • Led the Necrontyr during the War in Heaven
  • Made the pact with the C'tan
  • Ordered the Great Sleep
  • Recently returned from studying the Tyranid threat

Major Dynasty Overlords

Imotekh the Stormlord

Phaeron of the Sautekh Dynasty and perhaps the most powerful Necron Overlord currently active. Known for his strategic brilliance and the methodical expansion of his dynasty's territory.

Zahndrekh

Nemesor of the Sautekh Dynasty, a brilliant commander who still believes he fights in the wars of ancient times due to damage from the biotransference process.

Obyron

Vargard to Zahndrekh, his eternal bodyguard who maintains a careful watch over his master while executing his sometimes questionable orders with perfect loyalty.

Unique Characters

Trazyn the Infinite

The Collector, an eccentric Overlord who maintains vast galleries of historical artifacts and living beings in his world of Solemnace. Known for his wit and occasional intervention in galactic affairs.

Orikan the Diviner

A cryptek and master of chronomancy who can predict the future and manipulate time itself. His rivalry with Trazyn is legendary.

Anrakyr the Traveller

A unique Overlord who travels between tomb worlds, awakening his sleeping kin and uniting them against common threats.

Notable Achievements

  • The Silent King's return and unification efforts
  • Imotekh's conquest of the Ghoul Stars
  • Trazyn's preservation of countless historical moments
  • The World Engine incident

Ancient Technology

Necron Overlords have access to technology far beyond the understanding of younger races:

  • Resurrection Protocols
  • Phase Shifters
  • Chronometron devices
  • Mind transference technology
