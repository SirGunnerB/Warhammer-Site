Famous Naval Battles of the 41st Millennium

M31 - Isstvan III Atrocity

The Opening of the Heresy

Loyalist Forces Traitor Legions

The first major naval engagement of the Horus Heresy, where traitor vessels bombarded their own loyal brothers on the surface of Isstvan III.

Outcome: Traitor Victory

Loyalist Losses: Multiple Strike Cruisers
Traitor Losses: Minimal
M41 - Battle of Macragge

First Tyrannic War

Ultramarines Hive Fleet Behemoth

The climactic void battle between the Ultramarines fleet and Hive Fleet Behemoth, featuring the sacrifice of the Battle Barge Caesar.

Outcome: Imperial Victory

Imperial Losses: Heavy
Tyranid Losses: Near Total
M41 - Gothic War

Battle of Gethsemane

Imperial Navy Forces of Chaos

A massive naval engagement during the Gothic War, featuring the first deployment of Abaddon's Planet Killer.

Outcome: Pyrrhic Imperial Victory

Imperial Losses: Severe
Chaos Losses: Moderate
M42 - Indomitus Crusade

Battle of the Cadian Gate

Imperial Navy Black Legion

The catastrophic naval battle that saw the destruction of Cadia and the formation of the Great Rift.

Outcome: Chaos Victory

Imperial Losses: Catastrophic
Chaos Losses: Heavy
M41 - Orphean War

Battle of Amarah

Imperial Navy Maynarkh Dynasty

A devastating encounter between Imperial forces and newly awakened Necron ships, showcasing the technological superiority of the ancient xenos vessels.

Outcome: Necron Victory

Imperial Losses: Near Total
Necron Losses: Light
