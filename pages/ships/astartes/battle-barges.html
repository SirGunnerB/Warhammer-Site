Space Marine Battle Barges

The mightiest vessels in the Space Marine fleets, Battle Barges serve as mobile fortresses and launching points for planetary assaults.

Macragge's Honour

Chapter: Ultramarines

The flagship of the Ultramarines Chapter and personal vessel of Roboute Guilliman. This mighty vessel has served in countless campaigns and played a crucial role during the Indomitus Crusade.

Notable Achievements:

  • Led the defense of Ultramar during the Plague Wars
  • Pursued and engaged the Vengeful Spirit during the Battle of Calth
  • Spearheaded numerous fleet actions during the Indomitus Crusade

Vengeful Spirit

Legion: Sons of Horus (Former)

Once the flagship of the Warmaster Horus himself, the Vengeful Spirit was the site of the Emperor's final confrontation with his fallen son. Now it serves as the flagship of Abaddon the Despoiler.

Historic Events:

  • Site of the Emperor's battle with Horus
  • Led countless assaults during the Horus Heresy
  • Current flagship of the Black Legion

Invincible Reason

Chapter: Dark Angels

The flagship of the Dark Angels Chapter, the Invincible Reason has served since the Great Crusade. It houses many of the Chapter's most closely guarded secrets and serves as a mobile fortress monastery.

Notable Achievements:

  • Survived the betrayal at Caliban
  • Led numerous hunts for the Fallen
  • Successfully defended the Rock during multiple chaos incursions
