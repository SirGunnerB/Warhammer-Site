Imperial Cruisers

The workhorses of the Imperial Navy, cruisers form the backbone of most battlefleets and are versatile enough to operate independently.

Lord Solar Macharius

Class: Lunar-class Cruiser

Named after the famous Imperial Commander, this cruiser has earned renown for its actions in the Segmentum Pacificus. Its balanced weaponry and robust shields make it an excellent fleet command ship.

Notable Achievements:

  • Led the Pacification of the Maelstrom
  • Successfully defended three separate convoy routes from Dark Eldar raiders
  • Instrumental in the Suppression of the Macharian Heresy

Dictator's Fury

Class: Dictator-class Cruiser

A carrier variant cruiser, the Dictator's Fury has earned its reputation through its extensive fighter complement and their successful deployment in numerous void engagements.

Notable Achievements:

  • Broke the Ork blockade of Hydraphur
  • Fighter wings destroyed over 100 enemy attack craft during the Battle of the Maelstrom
  • Successfully escorted the Saint Celestine's Blessing during the Sabbat Worlds Crusade

Gothic Hammer

Class: Gothic-class Cruiser

A veteran of the Gothic War, this cruiser specializes in long-range engagement with its enhanced lance batteries. It has served as a flagship for numerous patrol flotillas.

Notable Achievements:

  • Destroyed three Chaos cruisers during the Gothic War
  • Successfully defended Port Maw's outer defense line
  • Led the pursuit of the infamous pirate lord Havoc

Tactical Roles

Line Engagement

Imperial cruisers excel in forming battle lines, using their broadside batteries to deliver devastating volleys while maintaining formation integrity.

Patrol Operations

Their balance of speed and firepower makes cruisers ideal for independent patrol duties and responding to threats across vast sectors.

Fleet Support

Cruisers provide essential support to larger battleships, screening them from enemy attacks while contributing significant firepower to the engagement.
