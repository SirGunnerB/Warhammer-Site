Imperial Battleships

The mightiest vessels in the Imperial Navy, these massive warships serve as the backbone of Imperial space warfare.

Eternal Crusader

Class: Emperor-class Battleship

The flagship of the Black Templars Space Marine Chapter, the Eternal Crusader has participated in countless crusades across the galaxy. Its most notable achievement was during the Third War for Armageddon, where it led the counter-attack against the Ork fleet.

Notable Achievements:

  • Led the Imperial Fleet during the Crusade of Fire
  • Destroyed three Ork Rok ships in a single engagement
  • Successfully defended the Shrine World of San Leor from Dark Eldar raiders

Divine Right

Class: Retribution-class Battleship

The Divine Right has served in the Gothic Sector for over two millennia. Its advanced targeting arrays and reinforced prow armor make it particularly effective in frontal assault actions.

Notable Achievements:

  • Veteran of the Gothic War
  • Successfully defended Port Maw from Chaos raiders
  • Led the Reclamation of the Cyclops Cluster

Dominus Astra

Class: Emperor-class Battleship

Famous for its last stand against Hive Fleet Behemoth, the Dominus Astra sacrificed itself by charging into the heart of the Tyranid fleet with its plasma drives overloaded, causing a cataclysmic explosion that destroyed much of the Hive Fleet's bio-ships.

Notable Achievements:

  • Heroic sacrifice during the Battle for Macragge
  • Veteran of countless engagements in the Eastern Fringe
  • Successfully evacuated three worlds in the path of Hive Fleet Behemoth

Imperial Battleship Classifications

Emperor Class

  • Length: 8-12 kilometers
  • Crew: 25,000-3,000,000
  • Main Weapons: Nova Cannon, Bombardment Cannons
  • Special Features: Launch Bays, Extensive Command Facilities

Retribution Class

  • Length: 7-9 kilometers
  • Crew: 20,000-2,500,000
  • Main Weapons: Lance Batteries, Torpedo Tubes
  • Special Features: Reinforced Prow, Advanced Targeting Systems

Apocalypse Class

  • Length: 7.5-10 kilometers
  • Crew: 23,000-2,700,000
  • Main Weapons: Heavy Lance Batteries, Macro-cannon Broadsides
  • Special Features: Enhanced Shield Generators
