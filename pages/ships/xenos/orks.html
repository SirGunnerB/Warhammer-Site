ORK VESSELS - DA BIGGEST AN' BADDEST!

From massive Space Roks to Kroozers cobbled together from space hulks, Ork vessels are as varied and unpredictable as the Greenskins themselves.

GORK'S FIST

Class: Mega Rok

Da biggest rok in Warboss Grimskull's fleet, this massive asteroid has been hollowed out and fitted with enough engines to "make it go real fast-like!"

SPECIAL GUBBINZ:

  • LOTS OF BIG SHOOTAS
  • EXTRA REINFORCED RAM PLATES
  • HOLDS OVER 10,000 BOYZ!

MORK'S REVENGE

Class: Kill Kroozer

Built from three different Imperial cruisers and whatever else the Meks could find, this vessel is a testament to Orky ingenuity and love of dakka.

KILLY BITS:

  • MEGA CANNON (STOLEN FROM DA HUMIES)
  • FORCE FIELD (WORKS MOST OF DA TIME)
  • FIVE DIFFERENT ENGINE ROOMS!

DA FAST ONE

Class: Terror Ship

Famous for its speed (painted red, of course) and surprise attacks on Imperial convoy routes. Has more engines than guns, which is saying something!

SPEED GUBBINZ:

  • ALL RED PAINT JOB
  • EXTRA BOOSTERS
  • LUCKY GROT FUEL MIXER

ORK SHIP TEKNOLOGY

DA WAAAGH! FIELD

Nobody knows how Ork ships stay together, but the collective belief of the Orks somehow keeps their ramshackle vessels functioning.

PAINT JOBS

Red ones go faster, yellow ones explode bigger, and purple ones are sneaky! Ork vessels often sport different colors for different tactical advantages.

MEK KNOW-WOTZ

Ork Meks have an instinctive understanding of technology, allowing them to cobble together working vessels from practically anything.

SHOOTAS AN' GUBBINZ

MEGA KANNONS

Massive guns that fire anything the Orks can load into them, from traditional shells to unfortunate grots.

TORPEDO LAUNCHERS

Usually stolen from other races and modified to be "more killy." Sometimes they even work as intended!

RAMMING PROW

Every Ork ship has one, because sometimes you just need to krump something up close!

ORKY TACTICS

GET STUCK IN!

Ork fleets prefer close-range engagements where they can bring their ramming prowess and short-range weapons to bear.

OVERWHELMIN' NUMBAS

A favorite tactic is to simply overwhelm the enemy with sheer numbers of ships, from tiny escorts to massive Space Roks.

SURPRISE ATTACK!

Despite their reputation for straightforward attacks, Ork fleets can be surprisingly cunning in their approach to void warfare.
