Eldar Naval Doctrine

Hit and Run

Eldar vessels excel at swift strikes, using their superior speed and maneuverability to attack from unexpected angles before withdrawing.

Fleet Engagement

When forced into pitched battles, Eldar fleets use complex formations and psychic coordination to outmaneuver their opponents.

Webway Operations

Eldar ships are uniquely capable of utilizing the Webway network for strategic movement and surprise attacks.
