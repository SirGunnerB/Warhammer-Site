Necron Technology

Living Metal

Necron vessels are constructed from living metal that regenerates damage over time, making them incredibly resilient in prolonged engagements.

Inertialess Drive

Allows for instant acceleration and deceleration, making Necron ships capable of maneuvers that would tear conventional vessels apart.

Quantum Shielding

Advanced defense systems that can predict and counter incoming fire before it hits.

Weapon Systems

Lightning Arc

Energy weapons that chain between multiple targets, causing devastating damage across enemy formations.

Particle Whip

Massive energy beams that can slice through multiple vessels with a single shot.

Star Pulse Generator

Capital ship weapon capable of destroying planets by inducing their stars to go nova.

Combat Doctrine

Harvest Operations

Necron fleets excel at systematic planetary conquest, using their superior technology to methodically strip worlds of life.

Fleet Supremacy

Their vessels' regenerative capabilities and advanced weapons allow them to engage multiple enemy fleets simultaneously.

Terror Tactics

The mere appearance of a Necron fleet often causes panic among enemy forces, as few have the means to effectively combat their technology.
